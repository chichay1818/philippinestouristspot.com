BURNHAM PARK

Burnham Park, located in the heart of Baguio City in the Philippines, is a sprawling urban park that offers a wide range of recreational activities and attractions for visitors of all ages. Named after Daniel Hudson Burnham, the American architect and urban planner who designed the city's master plan, Burnham Park is a popular destination for both locals and tourists alike.

The park features a picturesque man-made lake where visitors can enjoy boat rides, including pedal boating and rowing, amidst the scenic surroundings. Surrounding the lake are lush gardens and walking paths, perfect for leisurely strolls or picnics with family and friends.

In addition to the lake, Burnham Park boasts various facilities and amenities, including playgrounds for children, skating rinks, and biking areas. Visitors can rent bicycles or roller skates to explore the park's extensive grounds at their own pace.
