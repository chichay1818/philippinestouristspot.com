Tourist Spots in CAR Region



Tappiyah Falls

Tappiyah Falls is nestled within the picturesque Batad Rice Terraces, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its impressive engineering and breathtaking scenery. It's situated in a remote area, accessible only by trekking.


The area surrounding Tappiyah Falls is inhabited by the indigenous Ifugao people, who have been cultivating the rice terraces for generations. Visitors often have the opportunity to interact with the locals and learn about their traditional way of life.


Tappiyah Falls offers not only a breathtaking natural spectacle but also a chance to immerse oneself in the rich cultural heritage of the Ifugao people and explore the stunning landscapes of the Philippine Cordilleras.
