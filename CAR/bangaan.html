Tourist Spots in CAR Region



Bangaan

Bangaan is a traditional Ifugao village known for its well-preserved culture and architecture. The village is surrounded by rice terraces and offers visitors a glimpse into the traditional way of life of the Ifugao people. It's a great place to experience the local culture and hospitality.
