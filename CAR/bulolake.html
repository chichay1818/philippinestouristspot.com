Grand Zion Hotel and Resort

The Grand Zion Garden Resort is designed to melt into the surrounding hills of Tabuk. This simple hotel is ideal for those visiting the villages of Bugnay and Lubuagan.


They offer different amenities such as an access to their pool, meeting rooms, restaurant, banquet rooms, children activities, free parking, free wifi.
