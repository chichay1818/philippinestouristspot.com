BUSCALAN VILLAGE

Buscalan is a small village located in a some kind of a laid back town of Tinglayan, Kalinga. Tourists would often visit this village just to see and have their bodies tattoed by the legendary Apo Whang-Od who is considered as the last Mambabatok (traditional hand-tapped tattoo artist). Whang-Od and the rest of the villagers are members of the Butbut tribe and tattooing by hand-tapped is part of their unique and well preserved culture.


A tattoo in Buscalan costs around PHP 200 to PHP 50,000. The signature 3-dot tattoo from Apo, Grace, and Elyang costs PHP 200. On average, the small predesigned tattoos in Buscalan cost PHP 1,000 to PHP 1,500.

M Hotel is the newest hotel in town. Established in 2016, it stands out with its eye-catching boutique low-rise, white build.


Inside the hotel is a convergence of functional space and traditional Kalinga culture and arts. The lobby showcases well-maintained white interiors, accentuated by native textiles and hardware, rice grains, succulents, and timber.
