Tourist Spots in Cordillera Administrative Region- Apayao


HUTUNGAN (HIDDEN PARADISE)

Apayao province, dubbed as the "Cordillera's last frontier for nature richness", is sharing its collection of natural wonders to the world. With the advent of technology, such as smartphones and internet, going to Hutungan Hidden Paradise in Barangay Lower Maton, Pudtol, Apayao is easy.

The Isnag word for "big rocks" is "hutungan." It is a recently found tourism destination in the municipality that is ideal for visitors seeking refreshments and adventure.

Nestled at the foot of the mountain ranges and unpolluted river that flows from the watershed, this newly discovered tourist spot in Pudtol is a perfect destination for adventure-seekers and those who are looking for refreshment this summer.
