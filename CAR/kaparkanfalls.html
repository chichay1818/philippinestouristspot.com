Kaparkan Falls

Kaparkan Falls is a natural wonder in Tineg in Abra province, Philippines. It is a cascading waterfall surrounded by lush greenery and towering trees, creating a refreshing and picturesque environment for visitors.


This waterfalls Measure approximately 500+ meter high. It's literally a multi tiered high-terraced waterfall, filled with a number of small basins. The water in Kaparkan Falls eventually drains down to Tineg River. This one of its kind spring terraces of waterfalls can be best experience during rainy season.


Reaching this amazing falls is challenging, but it's worth. It takes you several hours to travel by bus or car from Manila then you have to take the hell ride by 6x6 truck from the town center going to the place and the ride is like a roller coaster by land. From the the jump off area you have to trek around 20-30 minutes depending on your pace to reach the falls.
