Tourist Spots in CAR Region



LUBO AND MANGALI RICE TERRACES

Lubo and Mangali Rice Terraces is located in Tabuk, Kalinga, Philippines. The Lubo and Mangali Rice Terraces in Tabuk, Kalinga. With a stunning backdrop of majestic peaks, these bright green terraces are Kalinga tourist spots that deserve a spot on any Pinoy's northern hit list.


The best time to view the rice terraces is during the planting season and when the rice terraces are already ready for harvest.

Ykalinga Hotel

This place is perfect for visitors who want a combination of peace, comfort and a convenient place.


They have different amenities such as parking, convenience store, laundry service, ironing service, air condition room, restaurant, free wifi.


Grand Zion Hotel and Resort

The Grand Zion Garden Resort is designed to melt into the surrounding hills of Tabuk. This simple hotel is ideal for those visiting the villages of Bugnay and Lubuagan.


They offer different amenities such as an access to their pool, meeting rooms, restaurant, banquet rooms, children activities, free parking, free wifi.
