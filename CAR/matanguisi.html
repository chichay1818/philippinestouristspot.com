Tourist Spots in Cordillera Administrative Region- Apayao


Mataguisi Spring

Mataguisi Spring is a natural attraction located in Kabugao municipality, Apayao Province, in the Cordillera Administrative Region of the Philippines. It is renowned for its crystal-clear waters, serene ambiance, and picturesque surroundings, making it a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking relaxation and natural beauty.

The spring emerges from underground sources, producing cool, refreshing water that flows gently into a small pool. The pool is surrounded by lush vegetation, including towering trees and tropical foliage, creating a tranquil oasis in the midst of nature.

Visitors to Mataguisi Spring can enjoy a variety of activities, including swimming, picnicking, and simply relaxing amidst the serene ambiance of the spring. The clear waters of the spring are perfect for swimming, providing a refreshing escape from the heat of the day.
