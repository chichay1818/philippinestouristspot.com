Art in Island

Art In Island is an innovative tourist attraction in Manila, Philippines, renowned for its interactive 3D art museum. Established in 2014, the museum offers a dynamic experience where visitors can engage with and become part of the artwork. The concept behind Art In Island originated from the desire to create a space where art is not only observed but actively participated in, breaking the traditional boundaries between art and audience. With over a hundred mural paintings covering walls and floors, the museum provides a canvas for visitors to unleash their creativity and imagination.

The history of Art In Island traces back to the vision of a group of Korean artists and entrepreneurs who sought to bring interactive art experiences to the Philippines. Drawing inspiration from similar museums around the world, they embarked on the ambitious project of transforming a warehouse into a vibrant space filled with optical illusions and three-dimensional artworks. Through meticulous planning and collaboration with local artists, the museum gradually took shape, attracting attention for its unique approach to showcasing art.

Since its inception, Art In Island has garnered widespread acclaim and become a popular destination for both locals and tourists. Its success lies in its ability to offer an immersive and engaging experience that transcends traditional museum visits. By encouraging active participation and interaction, the museum fosters a sense of wonder and delight among visitors, making art accessible to people of all ages and backgrounds. As a pioneer in the realm of interactive art museums, Art In Island continues to inspire creativity and spark joy in the hearts of its guests.
