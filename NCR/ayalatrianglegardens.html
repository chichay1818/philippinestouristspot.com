TOURIST SPOTS IN NCR




The National Capital Region (NCR) in the Philippines is the country's main economic and political hub, commonly referred to as Metro Manila. It comprises 16 cities and 1 municipality, including Manila, Quezon City, Makati, and Pasig. NCR is known for its bustling urban life, cultural diversity, and significant historical landmarks.
NCR is densely populated and is home to numerous businesses, government offices, shopping malls, and entertainment hubs, making it a vibrant and dynamic region.

AYALA TRIANGLE GARDENS

Ayala Triangle Gardens is a lush green space nestled in the heart of Makati, Metro Manila, Philippines. Spanning approximately 20,000 square meters, it serves as a serene oasis amidst the bustling cityscape. Managed by Ayala Land, the garden is meticulously landscaped with a variety of trees, plants, and manicured lawns, creating a refreshing environment for visitors to relax, unwind, and escape the urban hustle.

The garden is not only a tranquil retreat but also a vibrant hub of activity. It features paved pathways perfect for leisurely strolls, jogging, or simply enjoying a breath of fresh air. Throughout the year, Ayala Triangle Gardens hosts various events and activities, including concerts, art installations, and fitness classes, attracting locals and tourists alike to gather and partake in the community spirit.

Moreover, Ayala Triangle Gardens is surrounded by commercial and dining establishments, making it a popular destination for both recreation and dining. Visitors can enjoy a wide range of culinary options, from casual cafes to upscale restaurants, offering diverse cuisines to suit every palate. Whether seeking a peaceful respite or a lively social experience, Ayala Triangle Gardens offers a harmonious blend of nature, culture, and gastronomy in the heart of Makati.

NEARBY HOTELS IN AYALA TRIANGLE GARDENS

• The Peninsula Manila, often referred to simply as "The Pen," is a legendary luxury hotel located in Makati, Metro Manila, Philippines. Established in 1976, it has since become an iconic landmark renowned for its timeless elegance, impeccable service, and world-class amenities. Situated in the heart of Makati's central business district, The Peninsula Manila offers a convenient location for both business and leisure travelers seeking luxury and sophistication.

• Fairmont Makati is a luxurious five-star hotel located in the heart of Makati's central business district in Metro Manila, Philippines. Boasting contemporary elegance and sophistication, the hotel offers 280 spacious guest rooms and suites, each meticulously designed with modern amenities and stylish furnishings. Guests can indulge in a range of culinary experiences at the hotel's dining venues, including Spectrum for international cuisine, Raffles Long Bar for cocktails and light bites, and The Lounge for afternoon tea and evening cocktails. With its state-of-the-art facilities, impeccable service, and prime location near shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment hubs, Fairmont Makati provides an exceptional experience for both business and leisure travelers seeking luxury and comfort in Manila.
