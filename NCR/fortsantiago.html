NCR Tourist spots

The National Capital Region (NCR), commonly known as Metro Manila, is a bustling urban center in the Philippines. Comprising 16 cities and one municipality, including the capital city Manila, NCR stands as the country's primary hub for political, economic, and cultural activities. Characterized by a dense population and diverse demographics, Metro Manila serves as a melting pot of cultures, housing key government offices, corporate headquarters, and educational institutions. Despite its economic prosperity, NCR faces challenges such as traffic congestion and housing issues, prompting continuous efforts for urban development and infrastructure improvement.



Fort Santiago

Fort Santiago, nestled within the historic walls of Intramuros in Manila, Philippines, is a cultural gem that stands as a symbol of the country's colonial past. Originally built by Spanish conquistadors in the late 16th century, the fort served as a defensive fortress during the Spanish colonial period. Surrounded by massive stone walls and charming cobblestone paths, Fort Santiago offers visitors a captivating journey through time, providing insights into the complex history of the Philippines.


One of the key features of Fort Santiago is its iconic entrance, the Puerta de Santa Isabel, which leads to a beautifully landscaped courtyard. Within the fort, visitors can explore various historical structures, including the Rizal Shrine. This shrine, dedicated to the national hero Jose Rizal, commemorates his imprisonment before his execution in 1896. The fort also houses a museum that showcases artifacts, exhibits, and multimedia presentations, offering a comprehensive understanding of the fort's role in shaping the nation's history.


Beyond its historical significance, Fort Santiago stands as a serene and picturesque destination. The lush gardens and well-preserved structures create a tranquil atmosphere, inviting visitors to stroll through the grounds and imagine life within the fort's walls centuries ago. Today, Fort Santiago remains a popular cultural and historical landmark, drawing locals and tourists alike to explore its rich heritage and architectural beauty.


The Bayleaf Intramuros


The Bayleaf Intramuros in Manila is a distinguished hotel situated within the historic district of Intramuros. Offering a blend of modern luxury and colonial charm, The Bayleaf provides guests with an immersive experience in the heart of the Philippines' cultural heritage. With breathtaking views of Manila and the historic sites, this boutique hotel stands out for its elegant accommodations, fine dining options, and a rooftop deck that offers a panoramic vista of the city skyline and Manila Bay. Whether for business or leisure, The Bayleaf Intramuros provides a unique stay, surrounded by the rich history and cultural significance of Intramuros.


Stepping into The Bayleaf is like entering a sanctuary where the past and present harmoniously coexist. The well-appointed rooms are designed with a tasteful mix of contemporary amenities and traditional touches, ensuring a comfortable and immersive experience for guests. From the rooftop deck, visitors can savor breathtaking vistas of Manila's skyline, the historic walls, and the surrounding landmarks, creating an unforgettable setting for relaxation and contemplation.


The Bayleaf Intramuros also takes pride in its culinary offerings, featuring restaurants that showcase both local and international flavors. Whether enjoying a meal with a view at 9 Spoons or indulging in a casual dining experience at Sky Deck View Bar, guests are treated to a gastronomic journey that complements the hotel's commitment to providing a memorable and culturally enriching stay within the captivating confines of Intramuros.


The Manila Hotel


Manila Hotel stands as an iconic landmark in the heart of Manila, Philippines, embodying timeless elegance and a storied history. Established in 1912, it holds the distinction of being one of the oldest hotels in Asia, consistently welcoming dignitaries, celebrities, and discerning travelers from around the world. The hotel's grandeur is evident from its neoclassical architecture, adorned with intricate details that reflect a blend of Spanish, Filipino, and American influences.


Upon entering Manila Hotel, guests are greeted by a sophisticated ambiance, characterized by opulent interiors and impeccable service. The luxurious rooms and suites are meticulously designed to provide a harmonious balance between modern comforts and classic aesthetics. The hotel's illustrious history is evident in its halls, where photographs and artifacts chronicle significant moments, making every stay a journey through time.


Manila Hotel not only offers a haven of luxury but also boasts a range of world-class amenities. From fine dining at The Champagne Room to leisurely strolls in the beautifully landscaped gardens, guests experience a level of indulgence that reflects the hotel's commitment to providing an unrivaled hospitality experience in the heart of Manila.
