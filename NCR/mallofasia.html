TOURIST SPOTS IN NCR


The National Capital Region (NCR) in the Philippines is the country's main economic and political hub, commonly referred to as Metro Manila. It comprises 16 cities and 1 municipality, including Manila, Quezon City, Makati, and Pasig. NCR is known for its bustling urban life, cultural diversity, and significant historical landmarks.
NCR is densely populated and is home to numerous businesses, government offices, shopping malls, and entertainment hubs, making it a vibrant and dynamic region.

SM MALL OF ASIA



SM Mall of Asia (MOA) is one of the largest shopping malls in the Philippines and even in Asia. Located in Pasay City, Metro Manila, it spans an enormous area of over 4.2 million square feet. The mall boasts a vast array of retail shops, restaurants, entertainment facilities, and attractions, making it a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.

One of the main highlights of SM Mall of Asia is its iconic seaside location, offering stunning views of Manila Bay. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks along the bay area, catch beautiful sunsets, or even take a ride on the MOA Eye, a giant Ferris wheel that provides panoramic views of the city skyline and the bay.

In addition to shopping and dining, SM Mall of Asia also features an IMAX theater, an Olympic-sized ice skating rink, and a concert grounds where various events and performances are held. With its diverse offerings and prime location, SM Mall of Asia continues to be a premier destination for entertainment, leisure, and shopping in the Philippines.

NEARBY HOTELS IN MALL OF ASIA
