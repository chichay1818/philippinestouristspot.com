NCR Tourist spots

The National Capital Region (NCR), commonly known as Metro Manila, is a bustling urban center in the Philippines. Comprising 16 cities and one municipality, including the capital city Manila, NCR stands as the country's primary hub for political, economic, and cultural activities. Characterized by a dense population and diverse demographics, Metro Manila serves as a melting pot of cultures, housing key government offices, corporate headquarters, and educational institutions. Despite its economic prosperity, NCR faces challenges such as traffic congestion and housing issues, prompting continuous efforts for urban development and infrastructure improvement.



Manila Ocean Park

Manila Ocean Park, situated along Manila Bay, is a premier marine-themed park that offers a captivating aquatic experience for visitors of all ages. Opened in 2008, the park combines entertainment, education, and conservation efforts to showcase the diverse marine life found in the Philippines and around the world. The park's iconic Oceanarium, one of the largest in Asia, immerses guests in a breathtaking underwater tunnel, providing a 360-degree view of vibrant coral reefs, majestic sharks, and a myriad of colorful marine species.


Apart from the Oceanarium, Manila Ocean Park features various attractions such as the Trails to Antarctica, where visitors can encounter penguins and other cold-water species. The park also hosts educational shows and interactive exhibits, allowing guests to learn about marine ecosystems, conservation efforts, and the importance of preserving aquatic biodiversity. Additionally, the park offers unique experiences like swimming with dolphins and walking on the ocean floor, providing a hands-on and memorable adventure for those seeking a deeper connection with the marine world.


With its commitment to marine conservation, education, and entertainment, Manila Ocean Park stands as a prominent destination where visitors can marvel at the wonders of the ocean while gaining a greater understanding of the importance of protecting these ecosystems for future generations.


Hotel H2O


Hotel H2O, located within Manila Ocean Park, is a distinctive and innovative hotel that provides guests with a unique aquatic-themed experience. Designed with a modern and stylish aesthetic, the hotel seamlessly integrates marine elements into its architecture and design. The rooms and suites feature large aquarium walls, allowing guests to enjoy a mesmerizing view of underwater life, creating an immersive and tranquil atmosphere throughout their stay.


Each room at Hotel H2O is thoughtfully designed to offer a blend of comfort and sophistication, with contemporary furnishings and amenities. The hotel's commitment to providing a memorable stay extends beyond its aquatic-themed accommodations. Guests have access to Manila Ocean Park's attractions, making it a convenient choice for those looking to explore the marine wonders showcased in the neighboring Oceanarium, interact with marine life, and enjoy educational exhibits within the park.


Hotel H2O goes beyond the conventional hotel experience by offering thematic dining options, spa facilities, and even aqua-themed meeting rooms. With its strategic location, modern amenities, and one-of-a-kind underwater ambiance, Hotel H2O provides a truly unique and memorable stay, making it a sought-after destination for travelers looking to combine luxury with an immersive marine experience in the heart of Manila.


Hotel Jen Manila by Shangri-La


Hotel Jen Manila, part of the renowned Shangri-La Group, is a contemporary and vibrant hotel situated in the heart of Manila, Philippines. The hotel's sleek design and modern amenities cater to both business and leisure travelers, providing a comfortable and dynamic stay experience. With its strategic location near cultural attractions, shopping centers, and the bustling business district, Hotel Jen Manila offers convenience and accessibility to explore the vibrant city.


The hotel's well-appointed rooms and suites feature a blend of modern aesthetics and thoughtful functionality, ensuring a relaxing and productive stay for guests. From stylish furnishings to panoramic city views, Hotel Jen Manila aims to create a welcoming atmosphere that complements the energetic spirit of the city. The hotel's commitment to hospitality extends to its diverse dining options, meeting facilities, and recreational amenities, including a rooftop pool that provides a refreshing retreat with a view.


Hotel Jen Manila prides itself on its personalized service, embodying the brand's mantra of "Stay Happy." Whether guests are traveling for business or leisure, the hotel's friendly and efficient staff, coupled with its contemporary design and strategic location, make it a preferred choice for those seeking a modern and lively experience in the dynamic metropolis of Manila.
