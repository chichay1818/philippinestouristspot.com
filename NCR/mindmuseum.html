Mind Museum

Bonifacio Art Foundation, Inc. started planning for the first world-class home for science in the country in January 2007. Continuing thanks to private corporations and individuals whose contributions made it possible for The Mind Museum to open its doors to the public on March 16, 2012. The Mind Museum's mission is to initiate and bolster science engagement in elevating aspects of individual, community and national life to inspire solutions to real-world problems.

The Mind Museum is a captivating world of science and wonder: a realm of exploration, discovery, and imagination, where the boundaries of science and curiosity know no limits. With state-of-the-art facilities, countless exhibitions, interactive corners, shops and many more, The Mind Museum will surely satisfy the intellectual curiosity of children and adults alike.
