STAR CITY

Star City is a popular amusement park located in Pasay City, Metro Manila, Philippines. It first opened its doors in 1991 and has since become one of the country's leading entertainment destinations. The park features a wide array of attractions and rides suitable for visitors of all ages, ranging from thrilling roller coasters and water rides to family-friendly attractions and kiddie rides.

One of the highlights of Star City is its diverse selection of rides and attractions, catering to different interests and preferences. Whether visitors are seeking adrenaline-pumping thrills or gentle entertainment, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Popular attractions include the Viking Ship, Star Flyer, and the iconic Ferris wheel, offering panoramic views of the city skyline.

In addition to its rides and attractions, Star City also hosts seasonal events and performances, including concerts, magic shows, and themed festivals. These events add to the park's vibrant atmosphere and provide visitors with additional entertainment options. With its combination of thrilling rides, family-friendly attractions, and lively entertainment, Star City remains a beloved destination for locals and tourists alike, offering endless fun and excitement for visitors of all ages.

Nearby Hotel

Sofitel Philippine Plaza Manila is an iconic 5-star luxury resort nestled in an expansive tropical setting. Renowned for its magnificent contrast of captivating sunsets and the sparkling waters of Manila Bay, the hotel attracts international and local travelers alike with its signature Filipino brand of hospitality and the French joie de vivre reflected in its service rituals; from entertainment, food and wine offerings. Nestled adjacent to the historic Cultural Center of the Philippines, Sofitel Philippine Plaza boasts of a location within the city beat and the refreshing tranquillity of the bay. It stands 15 minutes away from the international and domestic airports and approximately 30 minutes away from Makati City, the central business and commercial district of thriving shopping, dining and entertainment venues.
