Tourist Spots in Region 1 Pangasinan




Agno Umbrella Rocks

Nestled along the rugged coastline of Agno, the Umbrella Rocks stand as a natural marvel, their towering limestone formations resembling giant umbrellas against the azure backdrop of the South China Sea. Carved by the relentless forces of wind and water, these majestic rock formations serve as a testament to the awe-inspiring power of nature, captivating visitors with their sculptural beauty and timeless allure.


Exploring the Umbrella Rocks is a journey into the heart of Pangasinan's natural wonders, where adventurers can marvel at the intricacies of geological formations and discover hidden coves and tidal pools nestled amidst the rocks. The rugged coastline offers opportunities for leisurely walks and scenic vistas, while intrepid explorers can venture into the depths of sea caves and grottos, uncovering secrets hidden beneath the waves.


After a day of exploration, visitors can retreat to the comfort of nearby accommodations, where warm hospitality and modern amenities await.

Nearby Hotels

Treasure Isle Guest House

Treasure Isle Guest House (Bolinao): Treasure Isle Guest House is a charming beachfront accommodation situated in Bolinao, just a short drive away from Agno Umbrella Rocks. Nestled amidst coconut groves and overlooking the azure waters of the South China Sea, this guesthouse offers a tranquil retreat for travelers seeking relaxation and natural beauty. The property features cozy rooms and cottages adorned with traditional Filipino décor, each equipped with modern amenities and private balconies offering panoramic ocean views.


Puerto del Sol Beach Resort and Hotel Club

Puerto del Sol Beach Resort and Hotel Club (Bolinao): Puerto del Sol Beach Resort and Hotel Club is a premier beachfront resort located in Barangay Ilog Malino, Bolinao, just a short distance from Agno Umbrella Rocks. Nestled amidst lush tropical gardens and overlooking the pristine waters of the South China Sea, this luxurious resort offers a tranquil escape for discerning travelers. The resort features elegant rooms and villas adorned with modern Filipino décor, each offering breathtaking ocean views and upscale amenities for a comfortable stay.


These accommodations offer various options to suit different budgets and preferences, providing a comfortable place to stay while exploring Agno Umbrella Rocks and the surrounding areas.
