Tourist Spots in Region 1 Pangasinan




Bolinao

Tucked away at the western edge of Pangasinan lies the picturesque coastal town of Bolinao, a hidden gem renowned for its pristine beaches, verdant landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. From the powdery shores of Patar Beach to the enchanting depths of Bolinao Falls, this idyllic destination captivates visitors with its natural wonders and timeless charm. Whether embarking on a leisurely stroll along the Cape Bolinao Lighthouse or immersing in the mystical allure of Enchanted Cave, travelers are invited to embark on a journey of discovery amidst Bolinao's breathtaking vistas.


Bolinao beckons adventurers and leisure seekers alike with a plethora of activities that showcase the region's natural splendor. Sun worshippers can unwind on the shores of Patar Beach, where powdery sands meet crystal-clear waters, while thrill-seekers can embark on exhilarating treks to Bolinao Falls, cascading amidst lush rainforests. History enthusiasts can explore the iconic Cape Bolinao Lighthouse, offering panoramic views of the coastline and beyond, while spelunkers can delve into the depths of Enchanted Cave, adorned with otherworldly rock formations.


After a day of exploration, visitors can retreat to the comfort of nearby accommodations, where warm hospitality and modern amenities await.

Nearby Hotels

Whether seeking opulence or simplicity, Bolinao's accommodations ensure a memorable stay amidst Pangasinan's coastal paradise.


Puerto del Sol Beach Resort

Puerto del Sol Beach Resort (Bolinao): Set amidst tropical gardens along Patar Beach, Puerto del Sol Beach Resort exudes luxury and tranquility. Guests can choose from elegant villas or deluxe rooms, all featuring modern amenities and private balconies overlooking the sea. The resort also offers an infinity pool, spa services, beachfront dining, and recreational activities.


Villa Soledad Beach Resort

Villa Soledad Beach Resort (Bolinao): A cozy retreat nestled amidst coconut groves, Villa Soledad Beach Resort offers a range of accommodations, from quaint cottages to family suites. Guests can unwind in the resort's lush gardens, lounge by the beach, or savor fresh seafood dishes at the on-site restaurant.


These accommodations offer various options to suit different budgets and preferences, providing a comfortable place to stay while exploring Bolinao and the surrounding areas. It's advisable to book accommodations in advance, especially during peak travel seasons. Additionally, travelers should inquire about transportation options to reach Bolinao from their chosen accommodation.
