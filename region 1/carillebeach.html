CARILLE BEACH

Carille Beach is a hidden gem along the coast of San Juan, La Union, offering visitors a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Tucked away from the main tourist areas, this pristine beach is characterized by its soft, powdery sands, gently swaying palm trees, and clear, inviting waters.


The beach boasts a serene and unspoiled atmosphere, making it an ideal destination for those seeking relaxation and solitude amidst nature's beauty. Whether you're lounging on the shore, taking a leisurely stroll along the water's edge, or simply soaking up the sun's rays, Carille Beach provides the perfect backdrop for unwinding and rejuvenating the mind, body, and soul.


Overall, Carille Beach offers a serene and scenic escape where visitors can reconnect with nature, unwind in a tranquil setting, and enjoy the beauty of La Union's coastline. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or simply a quiet day by the sea, Carille Beach promises an unforgettable experience for travelers of all ages.

KAHUNA BEACH RESORT

Kahuna Beach Resort- Nestled along the pristine shores of San Juan, La Union, Kahuna Beach Resort stands as a beacon of luxury and relaxation, offering guests an unparalleled beachfront experience amidst the stunning beauty of the Philippine coastline. The resort embodies the spirit of tropical paradise, with lush palm trees swaying in the gentle sea breeze and the soothing sound of waves lapping against the shore.


Accommodations at Kahuna Beach Resort range from spacious beachfront villas to cozy hotel rooms, each meticulously designed to provide the utmost comfort and luxury for discerning travelers. Guests can choose from a variety of accommodation options, all featuring modern amenities, elegant furnishings, and breathtaking views of the azure sea or the resort's lush gardens.
