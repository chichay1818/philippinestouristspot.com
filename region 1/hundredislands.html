Tourist Spots in Region 1 Pangasinan




Hundred Islands National Park

Nestled off the coast of Alaminos City in Pangasinan, Hundred Islands National Park stands as a breathtaking archipelago of 124 islands and islets, each adorned with pristine white sand beaches, lush greenery, and clear turquoise waters. This natural wonderland offers an array of activities for adventurers and nature enthusiasts alike, from exhilarating island hopping excursions to tranquil snorkeling adventures in vibrant coral reefs. Whether exploring iconic landmarks like Governor Island or immersing in the serene beauty of Quezon Island, visitors are treated to a kaleidoscope of experiences amidst the splendor of nature.


Beyond its picturesque landscapes, Hundred Islands National Park beckons with a plethora of activities catering to all interests and ages. Adventurers can embark on thrilling cliff diving escapades, discover hidden caves teeming with marine life, or simply bask in the sun-kissed shores of secluded beaches. Families can enjoy leisurely picnics amidst the island's lush foliage, while underwater enthusiasts can marvel at the park's rich biodiversity through snorkeling and diving expeditions. With its enchanting vistas and boundless adventures, Hundred Islands National Park promises an unforgettable journey into the heart of Pangasinan's natural treasures.


After a day of exploration, visitors can retreat to the comfort of nearby accommodations, where warm hospitality and modern amenities await.

Maxine by the Sea

Maxine by the Sea (Alaminos City): Nestled along the coastline, Maxine by the Sea offers a range of accommodations, from cozy rooms to spacious cottages, all with panoramic views of the sea. The resort features a swimming pool, restaurant serving local delicacies, water sports facilities, and guided tours to the islands.


Villa Antolin Hotel

Villa Antolin Hotel (Alaminos City): Conveniently located near the city center, Villa Antolin Hotel provides comfortable lodging options suitable for families and budget travelers. The hotel offers well-appointed rooms, a restaurant serving Filipino cuisine, and easy access to the city's attractions.


These accommodations offer various options to suit different budgets and preferences, providing a comfortable place to stay while exploring Hundred Island and the surrounding areas. It's advisable to book accommodations in advance, especially during peak travel seasons. Additionally, travelers should inquire about transportation options to reach Hundred Island from their chosen accommodation.
