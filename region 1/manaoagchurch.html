Tourist Spots in Region 1 Pangasinan




Manaoag Church

Nestled amidst the verdant landscapes of Manaoag, the Minor Basilica of Our Lady of Manaoag, affectionately known as Manaoag Church, stands as a beacon of faith and devotion for pilgrims and worshippers alike. Its Baroque architecture, adorned with intricate carvings and religious iconography, exudes a sense of reverence and spirituality, drawing visitors from far and wide to pay homage to the revered image of the Virgin Mary.


For centuries, Manaoag Church has been a site of pilgrimage and prayer, where the faithful seek solace, healing, and divine intervention. Pilgrims flock to the church to venerate the miraculous image of Our Lady of Manaoag, believed to have performed countless miracles and answered fervent prayers throughout the ages. The church's hallowed halls echo with the whispered prayers of devotees, creating an atmosphere of reverence and awe that transcends time and space.


After a day of exploration, visitors can retreat to the comfort of nearby accommodations, where warm hospitality and modern amenities await.
