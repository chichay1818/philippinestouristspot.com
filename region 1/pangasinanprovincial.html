Pangasinan Provincial Capitol Building and Capitol Grounds

Anchoring the heart of Lingayen, the Pangasinan Provincial Capitol Building stands as a testament to the province's rich history and cultural heritage. Its stately architecture, characterized by neoclassical design elements and intricate detailing, commands attention against the backdrop of manicured lawns and vibrant gardens. Surrounding the Capitol Building, the expansive Capitol Grounds offer a verdant oasis for locals and visitors alike, providing a tranquil respite from the bustle of urban life.

Visitors can delve into the past through guided tours of the building's interior, where ornate furnishings and historical artifacts offer glimpses into Pangasinan's storied past. Meanwhile, the Capitol Grounds host a myriad of cultural events, from concerts and art exhibits to festivals celebrating the province's diverse heritage.


After a day of exploration, visitors can retreat to the comfort of nearby accommodations, where warm hospitality and modern amenities await.
