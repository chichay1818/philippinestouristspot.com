PORO POINT BEACH

Poro Point Beach, nestled along the coastline of San Fernando City, offers visitors a serene escape amidst the natural splendor of La Union's shores. The beach is characterized by its pristine sands, crystal-clear waters, and stunning vistas of the surrounding coastline and rugged cliffs.


One of the highlights of Poro Point Beach is its tranquil ambiance, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and rejuvenation. Visitors can unwind on the soft sands, listening to the gentle lapping of the waves and basking in the warm tropical sun. The beach's secluded location ensures a peaceful retreat away from the crowds, allowing guests to reconnect with nature and recharge their spirits.


Poro Point Beach offers a tranquil and scenic escape where visitors can enjoy the beauty of La Union's coastline in a peaceful and relaxed setting. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or simply a quiet day by the sea, Poro Point Beach promises an unforgettable experience for travelers of all ages.
