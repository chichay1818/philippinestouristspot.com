Tourist Spots in Region 1 LA UNION




TANGADAN FALLS

The journey to Tangadan Falls begins with a scenic trek through verdant forests, crossing streams, and traversing rocky paths, adding an element of adventure to the experience. Visitors are treated to picturesque views of the surrounding mountains and vegetation, providing ample opportunities for photography and appreciation of the region's natural splendor.


Adventure enthusiasts can also indulge in activities such as cliff jumping, rock climbing, and exploring hidden caves around the falls, adding excitement to the experience. Meanwhile, those seeking tranquility can simply lounge by the poolside, enjoying the serene ambiance and tranquility of this natural oasis.


Overall, Tangadan Falls offers a memorable and immersive nature experience, where visitors can rejuvenate their senses, reconnect with the environment, and create lasting memories amidst the beauty of La Union's wilderness.

TANGADAN GREEN RIVER RESORT

TANGADAN GREEN RIVER RESORT - Tangadan Green River Resort is a charming eco-friendly resort nestled amidst the lush landscapes of San Gabriel, offering visitors a serene and tranquil retreat in close proximity to the stunning Tangadan Falls. The resort is known for its scenic location, sustainable practices, and warm hospitality, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those seeking relaxation amidst the beauty of La Union's wilderness.


VITALIS VILLAS

Vitalis Villas- Nestled along the picturesque coastline of Santiago, Ilocos Sur, Vitalis Villas is a lavish retreat that epitomizes luxury and elegance. The resort boasts a stunning beachfront location, allowing guests to bask in the beauty of the South China Sea while indulging in world-class amenities and personalized service.


Accommodations at Vitalis Villas are nothing short of spectacular, with each villa exuding opulence and sophistication. Guests can choose from a selection of spacious and beautifully appointed villas, ranging from one-bedroom to three-bedroom units, each thoughtfully designed to provide the utmost comfort and privacy. Featuring modern amenities and stylish furnishings, the villas offer a seamless blend of contemporary luxury and traditional charm.
