Tourist Spot in Region 10

Ardent Hibok-Hibok Spring Resort

The Ardent Hibok-Hibok Spring Resort is a renowned destination nestled in the picturesque island of Camiguin, Philippines. Situated near the foot of Mount Hibok-Hibok, this resort boasts natural hot springs that offer visitors a relaxing and rejuvenating experience.Surrounded by lush greenery and tropical landscapes, it provides a serene atmosphere for travelers seeking a tranquil getaway. With its clear, warm waters and scenic views, the Ardent Hibok-Hibok Spring Resort is a must-visit spot for nature lovers and those looking to unwind in the heart of paradise.

Paras Beach Reseort

Paras Beach Reseort,Nestled along the shores of Yumbing in Mambajao, Camiguin, Paras Beach Resort offers beachfront accommodation and breathtaking views of the Bohol Sea. The resort features spacious and well-appointed rooms and cottages, each offering modern amenities and private balconies or verandas overlooking the sea.Paras Beach Resort boasts a swimming pool where guests can take a refreshing dip or lounge under the sun on comfortable sun loungers.

Bahay Bakasyunan sa Camiguin

Bahay Bakasyunan sa Camiguin Located along the National Highway in Balbagon, Mambajao, Camiguin, Bahay Bakasyunan sa Camiguin offers a relaxing and enjoyable retreat for travelers visiting the island. The resort features spacious and well-appointed rooms, each equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable stay for guests. Some rooms offer views of the surrounding lush gardens or the nearby beach.
