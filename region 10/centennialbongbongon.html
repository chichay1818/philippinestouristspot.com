Centennial Bongbongon Marker

The Centennial Bongbongon Marker stands as a historic symbol in Gingoog City, Misamis Oriental, commemorating the centennial anniversary of the Philippines' independence. Located in Barangay Bongbongon, this marker serves as a reminder of the city's rich heritage and the struggles endured by its people throughout history.

The marker's design incorporates elements of Philippine culture and history, featuring intricate carvings and symbolic motifs that reflect the nation's journey to independence. Visitors to the Centennial Bongbongon Marker can explore its significance through interpretive displays and educational materials that provide insight into the events leading up to the country's liberation.
