Tourist Spot in Region 10

Dahilayan Adventure Park

Dahilayan Adventure Park, nestled in the lush mountains of Bukidnon, is a premier destination for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike. Situated amidst the breathtaking scenery of the province, the park offers a wide range of adrenaline-pumping activities and serene natural attractions for visitors of all ages to enjoy.

One of the main highlights of Dahilayan Adventure Park is its exhilarating zip line experience, known as Asia's Longest Dual Zipline. Spanning over 840 meters and reaching speeds of up to 90 kilometers per hour, this thrilling ride offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, including lush forests, rolling hills, and majestic mountains.
