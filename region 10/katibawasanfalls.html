Tourist Spot in Region 10

Katibawasan Falls

Katibawasan Falls is located in Barangay Upper Katibawasan, Mambajao, on the enchanting island of Camiguin in the Philippines. It is easily accessible by road and is a popular tourist destination on the island.The area surrounding Katibawasan Falls is enveloped in dense foliage and tropical vegetation, adding to the waterfall's natural charm. Towering trees, ferns, and other native flora thrive in the moist environment, creating a serene and tranquil atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy the soothing sounds of flowing water and bird songs while exploring the lush surroundings.

The pool at the base of Katibawasan Falls offers visitors the opportunity to swim, wade, or simply relax in the cool, crystal-clear waters. The natural pool provides a refreshing respite from the tropical heat and is a popular spot for visitors to unwind and enjoy the natural beauty of the falls.

Bintana Sa Paraiso

Bintana Sa Paraiso, Located in Upper Cabua-an, Mambajao, Camiguin, Bintana Sa Paraiso offers a tranquil and picturesque retreat amidst the natural beauty of the island.

The resort features cozy and comfortable rooms designed to provide guests with a relaxing stay. Each room offers modern amenities and stunning views of the surrounding garden or ocean.Bintana Sa Paraiso boasts beautifully landscaped gardens and lush vegetation, creating a serene and peaceful ambiance for guests to enjoy.

Paras Beach Resort

Paras Beach Resort, Nestled along the shores of Yumbing in Mambajao, Camiguin, Paras Beach Resort offers beachfront accommodation and breathtaking views of the Bohol Sea.

The resort features spacious and well-appointed rooms and cottages, each offering modern amenities and private balconies or verandas overlooking the sea.Paras Beach Resort boasts a swimming pool where guests can take a refreshing dip or lounge under the sun on comfortable sun loungers.
