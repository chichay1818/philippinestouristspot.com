Tourist Spot in Region 10

Malagos Garden Resort Chocolate Museum

Nestled within the scenic landscapes of Davao City, Malagos Garden Resort Chocolate Museum offers a delightful escape for visitors seeking a blend of nature, culture, and indulgence. Set amidst lush gardens and verdant surroundings, the resort provides a serene retreat where guests can unwind and immerse themselves in the beauty of the outdoors.

Visitors to Malagos Garden Resort Chocolate Museum can embark on an informative and interactive tour, learning about the origins of chocolate, from cacao bean cultivation to chocolate production. The museum features educational exhibits, multimedia presentations, and hands-on activities that engage the senses and ignite the imagination.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Malagos Resort Hotel

Malagos Resort Hotel-offers a tranquil retreat for travelers seeking relaxation and rejuvenation. Set against the backdrop of the majestic Mount Talomo and the scenic Malagos Garden Resort, this charming hotel provides guests with a serene oasis away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

From leisurely strolls through the picturesque gardens to adventurous zip line rides and eco-trails, there's something for everyone to experience and enjoy. Visitors can also indulge in delectable chocolate treats at the museum's cafe, savoring a range of artisanal chocolates and desserts made from locally sourced cacao beans. Guests have highlighted the exceptional staff, warm welcome, and accommodating service at the resort. Additionally, the resort allows entry for seniors, minors, and pregnant women, following health and safety protocols. Bringing in food and beverages is generally not allowed, except for bottled drinking water, as the resort has its own restaurant for dining needs.

Hotel Tropika

Hotel Tropika -offers a blend of comfort, convenience, and warm hospitality to discerning travelers. Situated just a short distance from the city's commercial and business districts, this boutique hotel provides guests with easy access to key attractions, shopping centers, and dining establishments.

It is highly rated for its comfortable and eco-friendly stay experience, excellent service, and facilities. The hotel has received positive reviews for its exceptional service, value for money, and well-trained staff, making it a top choice for travelers seeking a comfortable stay.
