Tourist Spot in Region 10

Mimbalot Falls

Mimbalut Falls is a stunning natural attraction located in Iligan City, Lanao del Norte, Philippines. Nestled amidst lush greenery and tropical forests, Mimbalut Falls is renowned for its picturesque beauty and refreshing ambiance.

The pool at the base of Katibawasan Falls offers visitors the opportunity to swim, wade, or simply relax in the cool, crystal-clear waters. The natural pool provides a refreshing respite from the tropical heat and is a popular spot for visitors to unwind and enjoy the natural beauty of the falls.Mimbalut Falls is situated approximately 11 kilometers away from the city center of Iligan City. It is easily accessible by road, and visitors can reach the falls via private vehicle or public transportation.Mimbalut Falls captivates visitors with its cascading waters, which flow gracefully down a series of rock formations into a crystal-clear pool below. The falls are surrounded by towering trees and verdant vegetation, creating a serene and tranquil environment.
