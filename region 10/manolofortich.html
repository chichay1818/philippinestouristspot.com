Tourist Spot in Region 10

Manolo Fortich

Manolo Fortich Bukidnon, Dahilayan Forest Park is nestled at the foot of Mount Kitanglad 4,700 ft. above sea level. Forest Park was built with the vision of helping develop the tourism industry in Bukidnon and the whole Philippines as well.

The park offers a wide range of attractions, such as the Forest Luge, an exciting go-cart toboggan ride with breathtaking park views. Visitors can also go on ATV rides through a 3.2 KM forest trail, while the hanging bridge offers panoramic vistas unique to Bukidnon.
