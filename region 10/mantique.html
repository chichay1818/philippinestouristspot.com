Tourist Spot in Region 10

Mantique Island

Mantigue Island is situated about 3.5 kilometers off the coast of Barangay San Roque in the municipality of Mahinog, Camiguin Province. It is accessible via a short boat ride from Mahinog or nearby towns on Camiguin Island.Despite its small size, Mantigue Island boasts diverse natural landscapes, including pristine white sand beaches, lush mangrove forests, and vibrant coral reefs. The island covers an area of approximately 4 hectares (10 acres), making it easy to explore on foot.Mantigue Island is renowned for its picturesque beaches with powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters. Visitors can relax on the beach, swim in the inviting turquoise sea, or snorkel along the shallow reefs teeming with colorful marine life. The island's calm and shallow waters make it an ideal destination for families and leisurely beachgoers.
