Tourist Spot in Region 10

Mapawa Nature Park

This is a vast property abundant with magnificent waterfalls , imposing views, and exciting activities nestled in the forest park in Cugman. It offers a relaxing environment for those seeking peace and solitude as well as a site for fun and adventure for kids at heart of all ages. Moreover, the place provide a relaxing environment to unwind specially to people who seeks peace of mind and solitude. It is also better if you have your own car to get there, public transportation aren't available on the way from the city proper but instead you can rent either taxi or habal habal if you don't have a means of transportation.

Mapawa Natures Park is located in Malasag, Barangay Cugman, Cagayan de Oro, Mindanao, Philippines. This park lies a mere 30 minutes away from the city proper of CDO. The place is perfect for trekking with nature, the park caters both team building activity and family bonding such activities like camping and reunion.
