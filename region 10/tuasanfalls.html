Tuasan Falls

Tuasan Falls is a stunning natural attraction located in the municipality of San Jorge, on the captivating island of Samar in the Philippines.Tuasan Falls is a waterfall located in Catarman, Camiguin Island, Philippines. It is known for its impressive water flow, surrounded by lush jungle, and featuring two freshwater pools at its foot. The falls have a 20-meter drop and are characterized by a powerful water flow, making them a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
