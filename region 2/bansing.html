Tourist Spot in Region 2

Bansing Falls

Bansing Falls is a stunning natural attraction located in the municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province, in the northern part of the Philippines. Tucked away in the rugged mountains of the Cordillera region, Bansing Falls captivates visitors with its pristine beauty and tranquil ambiance. The falls cascade gracefully down rocky cliffs, surrounded by lush vegetation and towering trees, creating a mesmerizing sight that is sure to leave a lasting impression.

To reach Bansing Falls, adventurers can embark on a scenic trek through the rugged terrain, immersing themselves in the natural beauty of the surrounding landscape. Along the way, hikers may encounter picturesque vistas, diverse flora, and the occasional wildlife, adding to the allure of the journey. Upon reaching the falls, visitors are greeted by the sight and sound of cascading water, as well as the opportunity to swim in the refreshing pool at the base of the falls.

Bansing Falls offers not only breathtaking scenery but also a sense of serenity and tranquility, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Whether exploring the surrounding wilderness, taking a refreshing dip in the cool waters, or simply enjoying a picnic amidst the scenic surroundings, a visit to Bansing Falls promises to be a memorable and rejuvenating experience for all who venture there.

Palaisdaan Hotel

Palaisdaan Hotel and Restaurant is a delightful retreat situated in the scenic town of Tayabas, Quezon Province, Philippines. Known for its serene ambiance and picturesque surroundings, Palaisdaan offers guests a unique blend of comfortable accommodations and delectable dining experiences amidst the beauty of nature.

The hotel features cozy rooms and cottages, each tastefully furnished and equipped with modern amenities to ensure a relaxing stay for guests. Whether overlooking the tranquil fishponds or surrounded by lush gardens, every accommodation at Palaisdaan provides a peaceful and idyllic retreat for guests to unwind and rejuvenate.

In addition to its comfortable accommodations and delicious cuisine, Palaisdaan offers various recreational activities for guests to enjoy, including fishing, boating, and leisurely strolls around the property. Whether seeking relaxation or adventure, guests can find it all at Palaisdaan Hotel and Restaurant, making it an ideal destination for travelers looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse themselves in the tranquility of nature.
