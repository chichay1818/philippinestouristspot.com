Lower Magat Eco-Tourism Park

Lower Magat Eco-Tourism Park is a picturesque destination located in the municipality of Diadi, Nueva Vizcaya, Philippines. Situated along the banks of the scenic Magat River, the park offers visitors a tranquil retreat amidst the beauty of nature. With its lush greenery, rolling hills, and pristine waters, Lower Magat Eco-Tourism Park is a haven for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

The park features a range of recreational activities for visitors to enjoy, including kayaking, fishing, and boating on the river. Adventurous travelers can also explore the surrounding hills and forests through hiking and trekking trails, offering breathtaking views of the landscape and opportunities for wildlife spotting.For those seeking relaxation, the park provides ample space for picnics and leisurely walks along the riverbanks, allowing visitors to unwind and immerse themselves in the serenity of their surroundings. Additionally, the park offers amenities such as cottages and camping grounds, providing comfortable accommodations for overnight stays amidst nature.

With its stunning natural beauty and array of outdoor activities, Lower Magat Eco-Tourism Park offers a memorable and rejuvenating experience for visitors seeking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and connect with the tranquility of the great outdoors.
