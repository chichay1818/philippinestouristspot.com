Tourist Spots in Region 3



Baler

Baler is a charming coastal town located in the province of Aurora, Philippines. Known for its beautiful beaches and surfing spots, Baler has become a popular destination for tourists seeking sun, sand, and waves. The town is situated approximately 272 kilometers northeast of Manila, making it easily accessible for both local and international travelers.


Surfing enthusiasts flock to Baler to ride its renowned waves, particularly at Sabang Beach and Cemento Beach. These beaches offer ideal conditions for both beginners and experienced surfers. Aside from its surfing scene, Baler is also home to several other tourist attractions. Nature lovers can explore the lush forests and waterfalls, such as Ditumabo Falls and Mother Falls, while history buffs can visit historical landmarks like the Baler Church and Museo de Baler, which showcase the town's rich heritage.


In addition to its natural and historical attractions, Baler offers a range of accommodations, from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxury resorts. Visitors can enjoy a wide variety of local delicacies and fresh seafood at the town's numerous restaurants and eateries. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or a glimpse into Philippine history and culture, Baler has something to offer. With its stunning beaches, natural wonders, and warm hospitality, Baler is truly a hidden gem waiting to be discovered.
