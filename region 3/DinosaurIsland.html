Tourist Spots in Region 3


Dinosaurs Island

Dinosaurs Island, located in Clark, Pampanga, is a unique theme park that takes visitors on a journey back in time to the prehistoric era. This educational and entertaining attraction features life-sized dinosaur replicas set in lush, tropical surroundings, making it an exciting destination for families, school groups, and dinosaur enthusiasts alike.

One of the highlights of Dinosaurs Island is its collection of realistic dinosaur statues, which are meticulously crafted to resemble the creatures that once roamed the Earth millions of years ago. Visitors can marvel at the sight of these towering giants, which include popular dinosaurs such as the Tyrannosaurus rex, Stegosaurus, and Triceratops, among others.

In addition to its impressive dinosaur replicas, Dinosaurs Island also offers a range of interactive exhibits and activities that allow visitors to learn more about these fascinating creatures. Visitors can participate in fossil digging activities, watch educational videos about dinosaurs, and even embark on a guided tour of the park to learn more about the different dinosaur species on display.
