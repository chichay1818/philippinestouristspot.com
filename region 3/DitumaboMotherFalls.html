Tourist Spots in Region 3



Ditumabo Mother Falls

Ditumabo Mother Falls is a stunning waterfall known for its impressive height and captivating beauty. The white color of the waterfall stands out against the lush green forest, creating a truly picturesque view. Visitors can take a refreshing dip in the crystal-clear pool and immerse themselves in the natural surroundings.


The waterfall is located in Barangay Ditumabo. To reach it, tourists can rent a tricycle or passenger van from Baler town, which takes around 30 to 45 minutes to reach Barangay Ditumabo. From there, visitors will need to continue on foot to reach the waterfall.


For accommodation recommendations, you can stay at Seasta Beach Resort, which takes around 40 minutes to get to Ditumabo Mother Falls. This hotel provides car rental so that guests can easily visit various tourist attractions.


Seasta Beach Resort - is a beachfront hotel with an outdoor pool near Sabang Beach. Its curated guest rooms and well-maintained facilities makes it the perfect getaway away from the crowd. The room offerings can also cater to solo travelers, small families, and even big groups.



RedDoorz @ Solid Rock Guest House Baler - is offering accommodation in Dipaculao. This 1-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom.
