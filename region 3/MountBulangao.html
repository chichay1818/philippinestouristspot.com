Tourist Spots in Region 3




Mount Balungao

Mount Balungao is a popular hiking destination located in Pangasinan, Philippines, known for its scenic views and relatively easy hiking trails. Rising to an elevation of approximately 382 meters above sea level, the mountain offers a manageable hike suitable for both beginners and experienced hikers. The trail is well-maintained and features lush greenery, making it a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.


One of the highlights of hiking Mount Balungao is reaching the summit, where hikers are rewarded with panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, including nearby towns, rice fields, and mountains. The summit also features a viewing deck where hikers can take in the breathtaking scenery and snap memorable photos. Additionally, there are several spots along the trail where hikers can rest and enjoy a picnic amidst nature.


Mount Balungao is also known for its therapeutic hot springs located at the foot of the mountain. After a rewarding hike, visitors can relax and rejuvenate in the hot springs, which are believed to have healing properties due to their high mineral content. Overall, Mount Balungao offers a memorable outdoor experience for nature lovers and adventurers alike, with its scenic trails and relaxing hot springs providing the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation.
