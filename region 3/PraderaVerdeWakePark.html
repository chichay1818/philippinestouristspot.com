Pradera Verde Wake Park

Pradera Verde Wake Park is a premier wakeboarding destination located in Lubao, Pampanga, Philippines. It is known for its world-class facilities and scenic surroundings, making it a popular choice for both local and international wakeboarders. The park features a 1.2-kilometer man-made lake specifically designed for wakeboarding, with state-of-the-art features such as ramps, sliders, and kickers that cater to riders of all skill levels.

One of the highlights of Pradera Verde Wake Park is its cable system, which allows riders to be pulled across the lake without the need for a boat. This makes it easier for beginners to learn and for experienced riders to practice their skills. The park also offers amenities such as equipment rentals, a pro shop, and coaching services, ensuring that guests have everything they need for a fun and memorable wakeboarding experience.

Aside from wakeboarding, Pradera Verde Wake Park also offers other activities such as kayaking, paddleboarding, and swimming. The park is set against the backdrop of the scenic Sierra Madre mountain range, providing a picturesque setting for visitors to relax and unwind. Whether you're a seasoned wakeboarder or a first-timer looking to try something new, Pradera Verde Wake Park offers an exciting and unforgettable experience for everyone.
