Tourist Spots in Region 3


Puning Hot Springs

The water here is known to be beneficial for the skin due to its healing powers. It is also believed to be excellent for relaxation, alleviating stress, treating sore muscles, and healing rheumatic pain.Captivating landscapes of Region 3 in the Philippines, Puning Hot Spring beckons travelers with its therapeutic waters and rugged beauty. Situated near the majestic Mount Pinatubo, this natural gem offers a unique blend of relaxation and adventure amidst its geothermal wonders. Puning Hot Spring is not only a haven for rejuvenation but also an opportunity to immerse oneself in the rich natural and cultural tapestry of the region.


Puning Hot Spring is renowned for its natural hot springs, which bubble up from deep within the earth, laden with mineral-rich properties believed to have healing benefits. The hot springs provide a sanctuary of warmth and relaxation, inviting visitors to soak away their stresses and tensions amidst the tranquil surroundings. Surrounded by lush greenery and dramatic volcanic landscapes, the hot spring pools offer a serene retreat where one can unwind and connect with nature's soothing embrace. Moreover, the geological features surrounding Puning Hot Spring, such as the remnants of the Mount Pinatubo eruption, add a sense of intrigue and wonder to the experience, highlighting the dynamic forces at play beneath the earth's surface.


In addition to its therapeutic hot springs, Puning offers an array of outdoor activities for adventure enthusiasts. 4x4 off-road tours through the rugged terrain provide an exhilarating journey, allowing visitors to traverse volcanic landscapes and scenic trails en route to the hot spring's remote location. Along the way, travelers can marvel at the stunning vistas of Mount Pinatubo and its surrounding landscapes, capturing the essence of the region's natural beauty. For those seeking a deeper immersion into the local culture, interactions with the indigenous Aeta communities offer insights into traditional practices and a deeper appreciation for the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity. Whether indulging in relaxation or embarking on outdoor adventures, Puning Hot Spring offers a holistic retreat that rejuvenates both body and soul amidst the enchanting landscapes of Region 3.


Nearby Hotels

There are accommodations available in nearby towns where travelers can stay. Here are some.


Clarkton Hotel- The Clarkton Hotel is a renowned hotel located in Angeles City, Pampanga, Philippines. Established in 1992, it has been providing exceptional hospitality services for over two decades. The hotel offers comfortable accommodations ranging from standard rooms to deluxe suites, catering to various preferences and budgets. Guests can enjoy a range of amenities including a swimming pool, restaurant serving local and international cuisine, bar, spa, fitness center, and meeting facilities. Its strategic location near Clark Freeport Zone makes it a convenient choice for both leisure and business travelers. With its warm hospitality and array of amenities, the Clarkton Hotel ensures a pleasant and memorable stay for its guests.





Oasis Hotel - is a well-known hotel located in the heart of Angeles City, Pampanga, Philippines. Renowned for its warm hospitality and excellent service, the hotel offers comfortable accommodations and a range of amenities to cater to guests' needs. From cozy rooms to spacious suites, the Oasis Hotel provides a variety of options for travelers. Guests can indulge in delicious cuisine at the hotel's restaurant, unwind with a refreshing dip in the swimming pool, or enjoy drinks at the bar. Additionally, the hotel features meeting and event spaces, making it suitable for both leisure and business travelers alike. With its convenient location and array of amenities, the Oasis Hotel ensures a pleasant and memorable stay for its guests.
