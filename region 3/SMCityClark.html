Tourist Spots in Region 3




SM City Clark

SM City Clark, located in Angeles, Pampanga, is a premier shopping destination in the region, boasting an impressive array of retail stores catering to diverse tastes and preferences. From fashion boutiques to electronics outlets, visitors can find everything they need under one roof. Additionally, the mall features a modern cinema complex where guests can catch the latest blockbuster movies in comfort.


Aside from shopping and entertainment, SM City Clark offers a variety of dining options to satisfy every palate. Whether craving local delicacies or international cuisines, the food court provides a culinary journey for shoppers to enjoy. Moreover, the mall serves as a vibrant community hub, hosting events and activities throughout the year, ranging from cultural festivals to live performances, creating a lively atmosphere for locals and tourists alike.


With its convenient location and comprehensive amenities, SM City Clark provides a complete shopping and leisure experience for visitors of all ages. Whether spending the day with family, friends, or enjoying a solo excursion, the mall offers something for everyone to enjoy, making it a must-visit destination in Angeles, Pampanga.


Nearby Hotels

Midori Clark Hotel and Casinoon - offers luxurious accommodations with elegant rooms and suites equipped with plush bedding, flat-screen TVs, and minibars. The hotel boasts a casino, a spa, multiple dining options, and a poolside bar. Both hotels provide convenient access to SM City Clark and are suitable choices for travelers looking for a comfortable and enjoyable stay near the shopping mall.
