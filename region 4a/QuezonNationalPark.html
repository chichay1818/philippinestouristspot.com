Tourist Spot in Region 4A

Quezon National Park

Situated at the foot of Mount Banahaw, Quezon National Park offers opportunities for hiking, camping, and nature exploration.

Quezon National Park stands as a testament to the Philippines' natural and historical heritage, offering a sanctuary for diverse flora and fauna while preserving the legacy of its past leaders. It serves as a vital conservation area and a place of recreation and education for locals and tourists alike.The park is home to diverse flora and fauna, including rare species of plants and animals. It also features picturesque waterfalls, caves, and natural springs, making it a favorite destination for outdoor enthusiasts.
