Tourist Spot in Region 4A

Emilio Aguinaldo Shrine and Museum

The Emilio Aguinaldo Shrine and Museum has a significant role in Philippine history as it was the birthplace of the Philippine Declaration of Independence. The Aguinaldo Shrine is an important historical site in Kawit, Cavite, Philippines. It was the ancestral home of Emilio Aguinaldo, the first president of the Philippines and a leader of the country's fight for independence against Spanish colonial rule.

The shrine, originally built in 1845 and reconstructed with limestone and hardwood in 1849, showcases Aguinaldo's mansion, spanning over 14,000 square feet and featuring antique furniture, motifs of the Philippine flag, hidden passages, and nationalistic decorations.The property includes the Liwasang Aguinaldo, a park constructed in 1997 with an equestrian statue of Emilio Aguinaldo and engraved walls commemorating the Act of the Declaration of Philippine Independence.
