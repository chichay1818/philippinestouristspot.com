Tourist Spot in Region 4A

Maragondon River

Maragondon River is a picturesque and tranquil river in Maragondon, Cavite. It is a popular destination for kayaking, boating, and other water activities and is surrounded by lush vegetation and scenic views of the nearby mountains.Maragondon River Cruise Maragondon, Cavite The invigorating ambiance of Maragondon River awaits you.. Experience the Maragondon.

The Maragondon River, along with other water sources like Salipit Stream, Mambog River, and Malibic-libic River, serves as a crucial water supply for the municipality. Additionally, underground water and springs are identified as potential sources for potable water if tapped economically.The river also supports a diverse marine life habitat, including fish, shrimp, and seaweeds, contributing to the local ecosystem's biodiversity.
