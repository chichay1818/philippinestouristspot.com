Tourist Spot in Region 4A

Masungi Georeserve

The Masungi Georeserve is a conservation area and a rustic rock garden tucked in the rainforests of Rizal. Masungi’s name is derived from the word “masungki” which translates to “spiked” - an apt description for the sprawling limestone landscape found within.

In this sanctuary, guests will be able to commune with nature, and reenergise themselves while doing so. Wander through our pathways for an encounter with wildlife and plant species. Enjoy light snacks before exiting the trail.Learn more about the history of the place introducing you to a community rich with heritage and life.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation


Momarco Forest Cove Resort and Hotel

Momarco Forest Cove Resort and Hotel in Tanay, Rizal, is a nature resort nestled within a forest, offering a serene experience surrounded by the natural beauty of the Sierra Madre mountain ranges.

This resort is known for its Clean, Green & Serene environment, providing guests with a deep spiritual richness unique to the areaIt is the only resort and hotel in Tanay to be fully accredited by the natural beauty of the Sierra Madre mountain ranges.

Momarco Forest Cove Resort features amenities like outdoor swimming pools, a full-service spa, and comfortable accommodations, making it an ideal destination for those seeking a peaceful retreat in nature

Bakasyunan Resort and Conferences Center Tanay

Bakasyunan Resort and Conference Center in Tanay is a serene vacation spot nestled in a 15-hectare forest property, offering a back-to-nature ambiance with abundant flora and fauna.

The resort overlooks the stunning views of Sierra Madre mountains and Laguna de Bay, providing a calming effect on visitors. It boasts well-appointed rooms, a swimming pool, and a conference center suitable for various events like business conferences, meetings, birthdays, debuts, and proms.The location offers a cool climate with windy mornings that counter the summer heat, making it an ideal retreat from urban life's hustle and bustle.
