Tourist Spot in Region 4A

Mount Makiling

Mount Makiling is an inactive stratovolcano located in the provinces of Laguna and Batangas on the island of Luzon in the Philippines. The mountain rises to an elevation of 1,090 meters above mean sea level and is the highest feature of the Laguna Volcanic Field.Mount Makiling is a prominent mountain in the Philippines, located in the province of Laguna. It is a popular destination for nature lovers and hikers, offering scenic trails and lush greenery. Surrounding Mount Makiling are various accommodation options, including hotels and resorts like Sol Y Viento Mountain Hot Springs Resort, Makiling Highlands Resort, and Rising Sun Hotspring Resort.

These establishments cater to travelers seeking a peaceful retreat near the mountain, providing comfortable accommodations and serene environments for a relaxing getaway.
