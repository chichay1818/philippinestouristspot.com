Tourist Spot in Region 4A

Mt. Marami

Another favorite among mountaineers is Mt. Marami in Maragondon, Cavite, aka "Mataas na Gulod Boulders". This mountain rises to an elevation of 633 meters above mean sea level and it offers an easy hike and scenic views - ideal for newbie mountaineers. Mount Marami is located in Barangay Talipusngo, Maragondon, Cavite and is accessible from Metro Manila via Cavitex and a drive to Naic and all the way to Maragondon.

The trails of Mt. Marami are less traveled, providing a serene and less crowded hiking experience for those willing to explore its beauty. Hikers can expect unexpected delights like morning sea of clouds and a speedy descent, adding to the allure of this picturesque destination. The mountain's landscape features composite rock pillars, contributing to its local name "Marami," meaning "many/plenty.".
