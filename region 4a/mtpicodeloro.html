Tourist Spot in Region 4A

Mount Pico De Loro

Mount Pico de Loro, also known as Mount Palay-Palay, is a prominent mountain located on the border of Cavite and Batangas in the Philippines. It stands at an elevation of 688 meters above sea level, making it the highest point in Cavite. The mountain offers a stunning view of the South China Sea, Cavite, and Batangas provinces.

Its name, Pico de Loro, translates to "Parrot's Peak" due to its distinctive pointed summit resembling a parrot's beak. The mountain features a monolith, a 50-foot high pillar of stone near the summit, which hikers can climb with some bouldering and rappelling. Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Club Punta Fuego

Club Punta Fuego is a luxurious beachfront property located in Nasugbu, Philippines, offering world-class amenities and breathtaking views. This exclusive resort features an outdoor swimming pool, three dining options, a golf course, and various recreational facilities like a spa, water sports, tennis court, and more. Guests can enjoy spacious suites and villas with balconies, satellite TV, and safe, along with exceptional service from courteous and friendly staff. The resort provides a serene environment for relaxation, with options for beach lounging, poolside activities, and dining at international and Italian restaurants.

Club Punta Fuego has received high ratings for its cleanliness, comfort, value for money, and overall experience, making it a popular choice for couples and travelers seeking a premium beach getaway experience.

Pico Sands Hotel

Pico Sands Hotel is a luxurious beachfront property located in Nasugbu, Philippines, offering a serene environment for relaxation and a range of world-class amenities. This contemporary seven-story hotel comprises 154 spacious rooms equipped with modern amenities and captivating views of the lush mountains and tranquil lagoon. The hotel features an outdoor pool, a fitness center, a luxurious spa, and various recreational facilities like water sports, bowling, billiards, and chess.

Guests can enjoy delectable local and international cuisines at the in-house Pico Restaurant and Bar, with the option for room service. Pico Sands Hotel provides a high level of service, including luggage storage, ironing service, and meeting/banqueting facilities.
