Tourist Spots in Region 4B




Pangulasian Island

Pangulasian Island is El Nido Resorts' eco-luxury island hideaway in Bacuit Bay, El Nido, Palawan. The resort is set fronting a pristine beach and against a backdrop of tropical forest. Also known as the "Island of the Sun", Pangulasian has breathtaking views of both the sunrise and sunset.
