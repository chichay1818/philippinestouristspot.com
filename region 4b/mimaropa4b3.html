Tourist Spots in Region 4B MIMAROPA: Occidental Mindoro


Tukuran Falls

Tukuran Falls, nestled in the lush forests of Occidental Mindoro, Philippines, offers a serene escape into nature's embrace. This hidden gem, located near the municipality of San Jose, presents visitors with a captivating landscape of cascading water amidst verdant foliage.


To access Tukuran Falls, adventurers embark on a trek through dense forests, following trails that wind their way through the natural terrain. Along the journey, the sounds of chirping birds and rustling leaves create a tranquil ambiance, heightening the anticipation of what lies ahead.
