Tourist Spots in Region 5


Binurong Point

Binurong Point, a hidden gem nestled along the eastern coast of Catanduanes Island in the Philippines, offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and the surrounding landscapes. This natural wonder remains relatively untouched, making it a tranquil retreat for travelers seeking solitude and natural beauty. The journey to Binurong Point involves a scenic trek through lush forests and rolling hills, providing opportunities to immerse oneself in the island's pristine wilderness and diverse flora and fauna.


Upon reaching Binurong Point, visitors are rewarded with stunning vistas of jagged cliffs, azure waters, and verdant hillsides stretching as far as the eye can see. The rugged coastline, dotted with rock formations and hidden coves, creates a dramatic backdrop against the vast expanse of the ocean. Travelers can explore the area at their leisure, marveling at the beauty of the landscape and capturing unforgettable moments amidst nature's splendor.


Binurong Point is more than just a scenic viewpoint; it's a sanctuary where visitors can connect with the natural world and appreciate the wonders of creation. Whether watching the sunrise paint the sky in hues of orange and pink or simply sitting in quiet contemplation as waves crash against the cliffs below, a visit to Binurong Point is a soul-stirring experience that leaves a lasting impression of awe and wonder.


Nearby Hotels

There are accommodations available in nearby towns where travelers can stay. Here are some.


Puraran Surf Beach Resort - located in Baras, Catanduanes, offers comfortable accommodations with a range of amenities to enhance your stay. The resort features cozy cottages and rooms equipped with amenities such as private bathrooms, air conditioning or fans, and stunning views of the ocean or surrounding gardens. Guests can unwind and enjoy the laid-back atmosphere of the resort, relax on the beach, or take surfing lessons at the famous "Majestic" wave break. The resort also provides amenities such as a restaurant serving delicious local and international cuisine, a beachside bar, Wi-Fi access in common areas, and outdoor activities like snorkeling, island hopping, and trekking.





Catanduanes Midtown Inn - Situated in Virac, the capital city of Catanduanes, Catanduanes Midtown Inn offers convenient accommodations with essential amenities for a comfortable stay. The inn provides cozy rooms equipped with amenities such as air conditioning, cable TV, hot showers, and Wi-Fi access. Guests can enjoy easy access to Virac's attractions, including historic sites, markets, and local restaurants. The inn also offers amenities such as a restaurant serving Filipino cuisine, room service, laundry facilities, and 24-hour front desk assistance to cater to guests' needs throughout their stay.
