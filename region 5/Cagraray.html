Tourist Spots in Region 5




Cagraray

Cagraray Island is a picturesque island located in Albay province, Philippines, known for its stunning landscapes and natural attractions. The island is renowned for its beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and breathtaking views of the Mayon Volcano. It offers visitors a peaceful retreat surrounded by nature's beauty, making it a popular destination for both local and international tourists seeking a relaxing getaway.

One of the island's most famous landmarks is the Cagraray Eco-Energy Park, home to the iconic "Stella Maris" chapel, which boasts stunning architecture and panoramic views of the surrounding area. The park also features zip lines, hanging bridges, and other eco-adventure activities that attract thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike. Additionally, Cagraray Island is known for its vibrant marine life, making it a fantastic destination for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts looking to explore the underwater world.

Cagraray Island offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and relaxation, making it a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to the Philippines. Whether you're looking to unwind on pristine beaches, explore the island's natural wonders, or embark on thrilling eco-adventures, Cagraray Island has something for everyone to enjoy.

Nearby Hotels

Misibis Bay Resort Spa - This luxury resort offers luxurious accommodations with stunning views of the ocean or the lush greenery. The resort features a private beach, infinity pool, spa, and multiple dining options serving local and international cuisine. Guests can also enjoy various activities such as water sports, ATV rides, and island hopping tours.

Villa Angelina Resort - Situated approximately 3 kilometers from the Mayon Planetarium, La Piazza Hotel offers comfortable rooms and suites designed for both leisure and business travelers. The hotel features a restaurant, a rooftop bar, a swimming pool, and a convention center. Guests can enjoy a convenient stay with amenities such as free Wi-Fi, parking, and a complimentary breakfast, making it a suitable choice for visitors to the Mayon Planetarium.
