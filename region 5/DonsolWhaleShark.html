Tourist Spots in Region 5

Donsol Whale Shark

Donsol, a quaint town located in the province of Sorsogon, Philippines, is renowned for being one of the best places in the world to see whale sharks, locally known as "butanding." These gentle giants, the largest fish in the sea, migrate to the waters of Donsol to feed on plankton, providing a unique opportunity for tourists to witness them up close. The whale shark season in Donsol typically runs from November to June, with peak sightings occurring from February to May.

The whale shark interaction experience in Donsol is carefully regulated to ensure the safety of the animals and the sustainability of tourism activities. Tourists are required to undergo a briefing session to learn about the proper conduct during encounters with whale sharks. Small boats, known as "banca," are used to take tourists to the feeding areas where the whale sharks can be found. Once a whale shark is spotted, tourists are allowed to snorkel and swim alongside these magnificent creatures, providing an unforgettable and eco-conscious wildlife experience.

Aside from whale shark encounters, Donsol offers other attractions and activities for visitors to enjoy. The town is blessed with stunning natural beauty, including pristine beaches, lush mangrove forests, and picturesque rivers. Visitors can also explore the local culture and heritage by visiting the Donsol Museum and engaging with the warm and welcoming locals. Donsol is truly a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike, offering a unique and unforgettable experience with the gentle giants of the sea.
