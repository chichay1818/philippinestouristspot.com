Tourist Spots in Region 5




Luyang Cave Park

Luyang Cave Park, nestled in the scenic municipality of San Andres in Catanduanes, Philippines, is a natural wonder waiting to be explored. The park's main attraction is its extensive cave system, which offers visitors a fascinating glimpse into the underground world. Inside the caves, visitors can marvel at the intricate limestone formations, including stalactite' and stalagmites, formed over thousands of years. Exploring the caves is a thrilling adventure, with narrow passageways and hidden chambers waiting to be discovered.

In addition to its caves, Luyang Cave Park also boasts picturesque surroundings that are perfect for nature lovers. The park features lush greenery, tranquil streams, and breathtaking views, making it an ideal spot for hiking and nature walks. The park's well-maintained trails offer visitors the opportunity to immerse themselves in the beauty of the natural landscape while enjoying a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Luyang Cave Park offers a glimpse into the rich heritage of Catanduanes. The park features exhibits and displays that showcase the island's cultural traditions and way of life, providing visitors with a deeper understanding of the local community. Overall, Luyang Cave Park is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the beauty of nature and the rich culture of Catanduanes.

Nearby Hotels

Puraran Beach - offers a serene escape with its golden sands, clear waters, and majestic cliffs, making it ideal for travelers seeking a peaceful retreat. The beach is renowned for its excellent surfing conditions, attracting surfers from around the world, especially during the peak surfing season from September to April. Apart from surfing, Puraran Beach is also perfect for swimming and sunbathing, surrounded by lush greenery that enhances its natural beauty. The lack of commercialization adds to its charm, making it a hidden gem for those looking to unwind and reconnect with nature.

Mamima BnB - is a charming bed and breakfast located in Baras, Catanduanes, near Puraran Beach. It offers cozy accommodations with a home-like atmosphere, perfect for travelers looking for a relaxing and comfortable stay. The BnB is known for its friendly hosts who provide personalized service and local tips to enhance your stay. Mamima BnB is an ideal base for exploring Puraran Beach and other attractions in the area, offering a peaceful retreat surrounded by nature.
