Tourist Spots in Region 5




Mayon Planetarium

The Mayon Planetarium and Science Park is located in Legazpi City, Albay, Philippines, at the foot of the majestic Mayon Volcano. It is a popular destination for both tourists and locals, offering a unique blend of education and entertainment. The planetarium features a state-of-the-art dome theater that showcases educational films about space, astronomy, and the natural wonders of the universe, providing visitors with a fascinating glimpse into the cosmos.

The Mayon Science Park also houses interactive exhibits, science-themed galleries, and outdoor recreational facilities. Visitors can explore the wonders of science through hands-on activities and experiments, making it an ideal destination for families, students, and science enthusiasts alike. The park's location at the base of Mayon Volcano adds to its allure, providing a stunning backdrop for a day of learning and discovery.

Mayon Planetarium and Science Park offers a unique and immersive experience that combines education, entertainment, and natural beauty. Whether you're interested in astronomy, science, or simply looking for a fun and educational outing, the Mayon Planetarium and Science Park is a must-visit destination in Legazpi City.
