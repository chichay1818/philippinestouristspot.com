Tourist Spots in Region 5


Mount Isarog

Mount Isarog, a majestic stratovolcano located in the Bicol Region of the Philippines, stands as a prominent natural landmark with a rich ecological significance. Rising to an elevation of approximately 1,966 meters (6,450 feet) above sea level, Mount Isarog is renowned for its lush tropical rainforests, diverse flora and fauna, and stunning biodiversity. The mountain serves as a vital watershed, providing water to nearby communities and sustaining a myriad of plant and animal species endemic to the region.


For outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers, Mount Isarog offers a plethora of opportunities for exploration and adventure. The mountain is crisscrossed by numerous hiking trails that lead to its summit and through its verdant forests, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape along the way. As hikers ascend the slopes, they may encounter a wealth of wildlife, including endemic bird species, colorful butterflies, and rare orchids, making it a haven for nature photography and birdwatching.


In addition to its ecological importance and recreational opportunities, Mount Isarog holds cultural significance for the indigenous communities that call its foothills home. These communities have established a harmonious relationship with the mountain, incorporating its natural resources into their traditional practices and beliefs. Visitors to Mount Isarog have the opportunity to learn about the cultural heritage of these communities through cultural immersion activities, such as village visits and storytelling sessions, providing a deeper appreciation for the mountain and its surrounding ecosystem.


Nearby Hotels

There are accommodations available in nearby towns where travelers can stay. Here are some.


Villa Escudero Resort-Located in Tiaong, Quezon, Villa Escudero Resort is situated relatively close to Mount Isarog and offers a unique blend of nature and culture. The resort features cozy accommodations in traditional Filipino-style cottages or modern rooms with amenities such as air conditioning and private bathrooms. Guests can enjoy activities like birdwatching, bamboo rafting, and cultural shows showcasing Filipino heritage. The resort also boasts amenities like swimming pools, a museum, and a restaurant serving authentic local cuisine.





Villa del Rey-Situated in the municipality of Pili, Camarines Sur, Villa del Rey is a relaxing retreat nestled amid lush greenery, offering a serene atmosphere for guests. The resort provides comfortable accommodations in spacious rooms or cottages equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, cable TV, and hot showers. Guests can unwind by the outdoor swimming pool, enjoy a leisurely stroll in the garden, or indulge in massage treatments at the spa. The resort also has a restaurant serving a variety of dishes, including local specialties and international cuisine.
