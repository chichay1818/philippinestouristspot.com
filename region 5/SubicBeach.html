Tourist Spots in Region 3


Subic Beach

Subic Beach, located on the island of Calintaan in Matnog, Sorsogon, is a stunning destination known for its crystal-clear waters and pristine white sand. Accessible only by boat, the beach is tucked away from the hustle and bustle of urban life, offering visitors a secluded and peaceful retreat. The beach is surrounded by lush greenery, adding to its natural charm and beauty.

One of the highlights of Subic Beach is its vibrant marine life, making it a popular spot for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts. The clear waters provide excellent visibility, allowing visitors to explore the colorful coral reefs and encounter a variety of marine species. Swimming in the turquoise waters is also a refreshing experience, perfect for =cooling off on a hot day.

Apart from its natural beauty, Subic Beach is also known for its iconic pink sand. The pink hue of the sand comes from crushed red organ-pipe corals mixed with the white sand, creating a unique and picturesque shoreline. The pink sand, combined with the azure waters and lush surroundings, makes Subic Beach a truly enchanting destination that never fails to captivate visitors.

Mango's Beachfront Resort Hotel - is a delightful beachfront accommodation located in Catarman, Liloan, Cebu, Philippines, offering a relaxing stay with stunning ocean views. The resort features comfortable rooms and cottages designed for a cozy beach experience, perfect for families and couples alike. Guests can enjoy a range of amenities, including a swimming pool, restaurant serving local and international dishes, and a beachfront bar offering refreshing drinks. Mango's Beachfront Resort Hotel is also a great base for exploring nearby attractions, such as the Cebu Safari and Adventure Park. With its picturesque location and warm hospitality, Mango's Beachfront Resort Hotel promises a memorable and enjoyable stay for guests looking to unwind by the sea.

Blue Rock Resort - is a popular beachfront accommodation located in Subic Bay, Philippines, known for its stunning ocean views and relaxing ambiance. The resort offers a range of accommodations, including rooms, suites, and beachfront cottages, all tastefully decorated and equipped with modern amenities. Guests can enjoy direct access to the beach, where they can swim, sunbathe, or partake in water sports such as kayaking and jet skiing. The resort also features a swimming pool, restaurant serving delicious local and international cuisine, and a bar where guests can unwind with a refreshing drink. With its picturesque location and array of amenities, Blue Rock Resort provides a perfect retreat for travelers looking to relax and enjoy the beauty of Subic Bay.
