Tourist Spots in Region 5




Watersport Complex

Camsur Watersports Complex, also known as CWC, is a premier water sports facility located in Camarines Sur, Philippines. This world-class complex is renowned for its wakeboarding and water skiing facilities, making it a popular destination for both professional athletes and water sports enthusiasts of all levels. CWC boasts a man-made lake with innovative ramps, rails, and obstacles, providing an exciting and challenging experience for wakeboarders. Visitors can also try their hand at other water activities like paddleboarding, kayaking, and jet skiing.


Aside from the exhilarating water sports, CWC offers a range of amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay. The complex features modern and well-equipped villas and accommodations that are perfect for families, groups, and solo travelers. The resort also includes restaurants, cafes, and bars where guests can refuel and unwind after a day of water sports. With its vibrant and energetic atmosphere, CWC is not only a sports complex but also a thriving community where visitors can connect with fellow enthusiasts and create lasting memories.


Beyond the water sports, Camsur Watersports Complex is situated in a beautiful natural setting, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic views. The complex provides a relaxing and serene environment, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the tranquility of nature. Additionally, CWC is located near other attractions in Camarines Sur, such as the stunning beaches of Caramoan and the famous Mount Isarog, offering visitors the opportunity to explore and discover more of the region's natural wonders.
