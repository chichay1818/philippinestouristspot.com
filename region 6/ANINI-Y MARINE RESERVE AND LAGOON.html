Anini-y Marine Reserve and Lagoon

Anini-y Marine Reserve and Lagoon is a protected marine area located in Antique, Region 6, known for its vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life. The reserve offers excellent opportunities for diving and snorkeling, with crystal-clear waters teeming with colorful fish and other aquatic species..


The lagoon provides a picturesque setting for kayaking and boat tours, allowing visitors to explore the tranquil waters surrounded by lush mangrove forests.


Conservation efforts are in place to preserve the beauty and biodiversity of this natural wonder, making it a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike.
