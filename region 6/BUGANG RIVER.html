TOURIST SPOTS IN REGION 6




Bugang River

Bugang River, located in Antique province in the Philippines, is a majestic body of water known for its enchanting beauty and tranquil surroundings. Flowing through the lush landscapes of Antique, Bugang River offers a serene escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike..


The river's clean and clear waters wind their way through verdant forests, creating a picturesque setting that is perfect for relaxing river cruises, kayaking, and even swimming.


The surrounding scenery of Bugang River is simply breathtaking, with towering trees and exotic flora adding to the river's natural charm.

Nearby Hotel

Situated along the stunning coastline of Antique, the resort features stylish and spacious rooms with modern amenities and breathtaking views of the crystal-clear waters.


Bugang Riverside Resort Inn on the banks of Bugang River, this resort offers a blend of comfort and adventure. Guests can choose from a variety of accommodations, from riverside cottages to spacious rooms with modern amenities.


The resort organizes exciting outdoor activities such as river kayaking, trekking, and birdwatching, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the natural wonders of Antique while enjoying the comforts of a well-appointed resort.
