Tourist Spots in Region 6

Carbin Reef

The Carbin Reef is a spotless white sandbar that sits off the shore of Sagay town in the Negros Occidental province. It is also part of the Sagay Marine Reserve, a 32,000-hectare protected seascape

Aside from its beautiful white sand, the reef's surrounding waters are teeming with wildlife so make sure to bring some snorkeling gear during your visit here. Underneath the waves, you will find a vibrant aquatic ecosystem filled with colorful schools of fish and other marine animals.

The best time to go to Carbin Reef is during the dry season in the Philippines, which typically starts in late December and lasts until the last weeks of May.

Since there are fewer chances of rainfall and tropical storms during these months, it is the perfect time to go snorkeling and sunbathing here.

NEARBY HOTELS

Nabulao Beach and Dive Resort

Nabulao Beach Resort is situated in Hinoba-an in the Visayas Region. Located in a protected bay, the resort has two outdoor pools and views of the sea, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar and Free WiFi is available in common areas and free private parking is available on site.

Spacious air-conditioned rooms include a flat-screen cable TV, safety deposit box and minibar. Some rooms include a outside seating area where you can relax while other units open up to views of the pool or garden. Private bathrooms come with a shower and toiletries are provided.

There is a shared lounge as well as a diving centre at the property. The resort also offers a selection of activities such as Scuba Schools International Diving courses, Try Scuba Dive Experiences, snorkeling, banana boat, kayaking, Stand up paddle boarding and island hopping. Guests can enjoy the sunset and delectable meals served at Capiz Shells, the in-house open air restaurant facing the cove.
