Gigante Island

Gigante Island, also known as Isla de Gigantes, is a group of islands located off the coast of Carles in Northern Iloilo. It's renowned for its pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and stunning rock formations.


The main islands are Gigante Norte (North Gigante) and Gigante Sur (South Gigante), both offering picturesque landscapes and excellent opportunities for swimming, snorkeling, diving, and island hopping. Gigante Island is also famous for its fresh seafood, particularly scallops, which are abundant in the surrounding waters and served in various local dishes.


With its natural beauty and laid-back atmosphere, Gigante Island is a haven for travelers seeking relaxation and adventure amidst a tropical paradise.
