Tourist Spots in Region 6




Hinigaran

Hinigaran is a municipality located in the southern part of Negros Occidental, Philippines. It is known for its scenic landscapes, rich agricultural heritage, and warm hospitality.


The town is nestled amidst verdant fields and rolling hills, offering a picturesque countryside atmosphere. One of Hinigaran's notable attractions is the stunning Mag-aso Falls, a cascading waterfall surrounded by lush vegetation, ideal for picnics and nature walks.


The town is also famous for its vibrant festivals, such as the Hinugyaw Festival celebrated every January, showcasing colorful parades, cultural performances, and local delicacies. With its natural beauty and cultural heritage, Hinigaran offers visitors a tranquil retreat away from the bustling city life.
