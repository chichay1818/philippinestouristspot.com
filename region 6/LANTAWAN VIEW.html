TOURIST SPOTS IN REGION 6




Lantawan View

Lantawan View in Negros Occidental is a hidden gem offering unparalleled vistas of the surrounding natural beauty.


Perched atop rolling hills, this scenic overlook provides visitors with sweeping panoramas of lush mountains, verdant valleys, and the sparkling waters of Negros Occidental.


Whether you're an avid photographer capturing the stunning sunrise or a nature enthusiast seeking tranquility, Lantawan View offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Nearby Hotel

For accommodations nearby, travelers have a couple of enticing options.


Sugarland Hotel, Nestled in the heart of Bacolod City, Sugarland Hotel offers a blend of modern comforts and traditional Filipino hospitality. With spacious rooms boasting city or mountain views, guests can unwind in style after a day of exploration. The hotel features amenities such as a swimming pool, fitness center, and an on-site restaurant serving delectable local and international cuisine.


Nature's Village Resort, Located in the charming city of Talisay, Nature's Village Resort immerses guests in a tranquil oasis surrounded by lush greenery. Set amidst a sprawling tropical garden, the resort offers rustic yet comfortable cottages and villas ideal for those seeking a peaceful retreat.


Guests can rejuvenate with a dip in the pool, indulge in spa treatments, or embark on outdoor adventures such as hiking or birdwatching in the nearby nature reserves.
