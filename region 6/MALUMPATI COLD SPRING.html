TOURIST SPOTS IN REGION 6




Malumpati Cold Spring

Malumpati Cold Spring is a hidden gem located in the beautiful province of Antique in the Philippines. Nestled in the town of Pandan, this natural wonder is a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking relaxation and rejuvenation in a serene setting. The main attraction of Malumpati Cold Spring is its crystal-clear waters that come from the surrounding mountains, creating a refreshing and invigorating experience for visitors. The spring maintains a cool temperature throughout the year, making it the perfect place to escape the heat and enjoy a peaceful swim..


The main attraction of Malumpati Cold Spring is its inviting turquoise pools, where visitors can take a dip and revel in the invigorating waters. Fed by underground springs, the pools maintain a consistently cool temperature, making them an ideal respite from the sweltering Philippine climate. Whether lounging on the banks or swimming in the refreshing waters, guests can bask in the beauty of their surroundings, with the gentle rustle of leaves and chirping of birds providing a soothing soundtrack to their experience.


Malumpati Cold Spring stands as a beloved destination where visitors can connect with nature and create cherished memories amidst its tranquil waters.
