TOURIST SPOTS IN REGION 6




Mararison Island

Mararison Island, also known as Malalison Island, is a hidden gem located in Antique province, Philippines. This small island boasts pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding ocean.


Mararison Island is a paradise for beach lovers and outdoor enthusiasts seeking a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.


The island's rugged cliffs, lush greenery, and tranquil ambiance make it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventurers looking to explore the untouched beauty of the Philippines.

Nearby Hotel

There may not be hotels directly at Mararison Island, there are accommodations available in nearby towns where travelers can stay. Here are some options.


Sampaguita Gardens in Antique is a charming and serene destination nestled in the heart of Antique province, offering a tranquil retreat amidst lush gardens and picturesque surroundings. This enchanting garden resort is a haven for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Guests can wander through the beautifully landscaped gardens adorned with colorful flowers, towering trees, and winding pathways, creating a serene and rejuvenating ambiance.


Mararison Beach Resort Nestled amidst lush greenery, Mararison Beach Resort boasts charming cottages overlooking the crystal-clear waters of the sea.


With its tranquil ambiance and personalized service, this resort provides guests with a relaxing retreat. Visitors can indulge in water activities such as snorkeling, diving, and island hopping, or simply unwind on the pristine sandy beach.
