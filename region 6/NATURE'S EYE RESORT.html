Tourist Spots in Region 6




Nature's Eye Resort

Nature's Eye Resort is a stunning eco-friendly retreat nestled on the beautiful island of Guimaras in the Philippines. Surrounded by lush tropical foliage and offering breathtaking views of the ocean, this resort provides guests with a tranquil and immersive experience in nature..


Offers a range of activities for guests to enjoy, including snorkeling, kayaking, and island hopping, allowing them to explore the pristine marine ecosystem of Guimaras.


Guests can also relax by the infinity pool overlooking the ocean or indulge in rejuvenating spa treatments inspired by local traditions..

Nearby Hotels

There may not be hotels directly at Nature's Eye Resort, there are accommodations available in nearby towns where travelers can stay. Here are some options


Costa Aguada Island Resort offers rustic cottages and beachfront villas. It's approximately a 30-minute boat ride followed by a short drive from Nature's Eye Resort..


Raymen Beach Resort offers beachfront accommodations with cottages and rooms. It's about a 20-minute drive from Nature's Eye Resort..


These accommodations offer various amenities and are located in close proximity to Nature's Eye Resort, allowing you to explore the area while enjoying a comfortable stay. Be sure to check availability and book in advance, especially during peak travel seasons..
