Tourist Spots in Region 6




Nelly Garden

Nelly's Garden is a historic mansion located in Iloilo City, Philippines. Originally built in the early 1920s, it was the residence of the Lopez family and was later transformed into a restaurant and events venue. The mansion retains its old-world charm with its elegant architecture, antique furnishings, and lush gardens surrounding the property.


Visitors to Nelly's Garden can enjoy dining in a refined yet relaxed atmosphere, surrounded by beautiful greenery and vintage decor. The restaurant offers a menu of Filipino and international dishes, often featuring locally sourced ingredients.


Additionally, the venue is a popular choice for weddings, special occasions, and corporate events, providing a picturesque backdrop for memorable celebrations. Nelly's Garden is not only a dining destination but also a cultural landmark that showcases the rich heritage of Iloilo City.
