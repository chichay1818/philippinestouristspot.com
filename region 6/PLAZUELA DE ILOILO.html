Tourist Spots in Region 6




Plazuela De Iloilo

Plazuela de Iloilo is a charming commercial complex located in Mandurriao, Iloilo City. It's known for its Spanish-inspired architecture and vibrant atmosphere. The plaza features a variety of shops, restaurants, cafes, and entertainment venues, making it a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.


Visitors can enjoy leisurely strolls along its cobblestone pathways, dine al fresco in its open-air restaurants, or shop for souvenirs and local handicrafts. Plazuela de Iloilo often hosts events, concerts, and cultural performances, adding to its lively ambiance.


With its blend of colonial charm and modern amenities, Plazuela de Iloilo offers a unique shopping and dining experience in the heart of Iloilo City.
