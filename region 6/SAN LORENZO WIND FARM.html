Tourist Spots in Region 6




San Lorenzo Wind Farm

San Lorenzo Wind Farm in Guimaras is a pioneering renewable energy project located in the municipality of San Lorenzo. It consists of several towering wind turbines spread across the picturesque landscape, harnessing the region's strong winds to generate clean and sustainable electricity.


The wind farm not only contributes to reducing carbon emissions but also serves as a symbol of Guimaras' commitment to environmental sustainability and green energy initiatives..


Visitors to the area can witness the majestic sight of the wind turbines in action while appreciating the natural beauty of the island province.
