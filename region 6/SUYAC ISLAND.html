Tourist Spots in Region 6




Suyac Island

Suyac Island, nestled off the coast of Negros Occidental, Philippines, is a hidden gem boasting pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush greenery. This enchanting island offers a serene retreat away from the hustle and bustle of city life, inviting travelers to unwind and reconnect with nature.


Suyac Island is a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. Explore its vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful marine life through snorkeling or diving expeditions. Embark on leisurely hikes through the island's verdant forests, where you may encounter rare species of flora and fauna.


Immerse yourself in the local culture by interacting with the friendly islanders, who are known for their warm hospitality and traditional way of life.

Nearby Hotels
