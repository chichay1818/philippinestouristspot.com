The Ruins

Altavas Veteran's Memorial Shrine located in the upland area of Brgy. Lumaynay, developed and funded by the Altavas Historical Research Society Park. The park has been declared as the number one "Pasyalanng Bayan" by the LGU.

The site was the exact location of the Headquarters of the Philippine Army during World War II with General Grasphil as a Commanding Officer. The Establishment of the Veterans' Memorial Obelisk tower faces the lusty vegetation of natural forest of Lumaynay and offers a breath taking view of Tinagong Dagat. The park is currently undergoing more developments, with gazebos being constructed connected by concrete pathways to the center of the park.
