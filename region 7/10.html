Tourist Spot in Region 7

10,000 Roses

The 10,000 Roses Café & More is located in Cordova, Cebu, Philippines.The main attraction is the presence of 10,000 artificial roses in front of the café.The roses are illuminated by LED lights, creating a romantic and enchanting atmosphere, especially at night.The owner was inspired by the Garden of Light at Dongdaemun Design Plaza in South Korea.

Visitors can enjoy the beautiful rose garden, witness the sunset, and dine at the café.The café offers food, drinks, and souvenirs.It is a popular destination for couples and tourists looking for a romantic setting.10,000 Roses Café & More is a unique and visually stunning attraction in Cordova, Cebu, featuring a large display of artificial, illuminated roses that create a romantic and enchanting atmosphere for visitors.
