Tarsier Sanctuary

Tarsier Sanctuary, located in Corella, Bohol, Philippines, is a vital institution dedicated to the preservation and scientific research of the endemic tarsier species. Established as the Philippine Tarsier and Wildlife Sanctuary of Canapnapan, Corella Bohol, this project aims to protect the Philippine Tarsier and its habitat from extinction through increased environmental awareness, research programs, and the establishment of protected areas for wildlife in Bohol.

The sanctuary, managed by the Philippine Tarsier Foundation, Inc., focuses on conservation, eco-tourism, environmental awareness, and research to safeguard the tarsier population in Bohol. Through initiatives like habitat conservation, eco-tourism development, and educational programs, the sanctuary plays a crucial role in preserving the unique tarsier species and promoting sustainable livelihood opportunities for local communities. Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Water to Forest Ecolodge

The Water to Forest Ecolodge is a unique and tranquil retreat located in Loboc, Bohol, Philippines. Nestled between the serene Loboc River and the lush surrounding forests, this ecolodge offers guests a truly immersive experience in nature.

The ecolodge features accommodations predominantly made of bamboo, utilizing local materials and traditional construction techniques. The rooms provide a rustic yet comfortable ambiance, with amenities such as air conditioning, private terraces, and scenic views of the river and forest. The ecolodge's design seamlessly blends with the natural environment, allowing guests to fully immerse themselves in the beauty of their surroundings.

Southside Tourist Inn

Southside Tourist Inn is a charming guesthouse located in Loboc, Bohol, Philippines, offering guests comfort and convenience in a picturesque setting. Situated in close proximity to popular attractions like the Tarsier Conservation Area and Hinagdanan Cave, this inn provides a cozy retreat for travel.

The inn offers a range of room options designed to cater to different preferences, ensuring a comfortable stay for guests. With amenities like free WiFi, a garden area, and cozy rooms, Southside Tourist Inn provides a welcoming environment for visitors to relax and unwind.
