Tourist Spot in Region 7

Bais Islang

Dolphin and whale watching is one of the well known tourist attractions in Negros Oriental. The main area that offers dolphin watching tours in Negros is Bais City; which is located on the east coast of Negros island.Most tourists who visit Bais would combine their dolphin and whale watching with a stay on the Manjuyod Sandbar.

Most would avail both Dolphin Watching and Manjuyod Sandbar Tour as a full day-trip package while some would also avail an overnight stay in one of the cottages / stilt houses at Manjuyod Sandbar.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Blue Orchid Resort

Our beautiful, private, seaside resort accommodation is tucked away in a secluded corner of Mac Payong, just 30 meters from the 2km White Beach.Just a short drive away from Moalboal and Panagsama, it offers a quiet, idyllic setting for your holiday. The location couldn't get any better for diving as one of the best reefs in the area is directly in front of your ocean view room. The reef is perfect for beginner divers, and also for snorkelers. However the step walls make an exhilarating dive for seasoned advanced divers.The resort's architecture is a blend of Mediterranean and native styles incorporating local stone and thatched roof with white arches and Spanish tiles. Each room has either a large arched doorway or huge glass sliding doors to provide guests with amazing ocean views.

Thalatta Beach & Dive Resort

Thalatta Beach & Dive Resort, a resort, hotel, restautant, located 25 kms from Dumaguete, in Maluay, close to Malatapay, Zamboanguita. We are facing Apo Island, one of the most famous diving spots in the Philippines.It only takes 15 minutes to Apo Island from the resort.

Thalatta Beach & Dive Resort is a tropical haven located in Maluay, Zamboanguita, Philippines, facing the renowned diving destination of Apo Island. The resort offers a comfortable and family-friendly retreat nestled in a lush tropical garden, providing guests with a serene and relaxing environment. With 16 rooms, the resort features a dive center on-site, making it a convenient choice for diving enthusiasts seeking to explore the rich marine biodiversity of the region.
