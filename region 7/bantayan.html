Bantayan Island

Bantayan Island is located in the Visayan Sea, situated to the west of the northern end of Cebu island, across the Ta√±on Strait.The island has an area of 108.77 square kilometers and is mostly covered with coconut palms.The elevation is mostly below 10 meters, with only one taller hill at 26 meters.The island is part of the Bantayan island group, which includes numerous smaller islands and islets around the southwest corner of the main island.

Bantayan Island is known for its pristine white sand beaches and clear waters, making it a popular tourist destination.The main town of Santa Fe is the center of tourism on the island.Popular activities include swimming, snorkeling, island hopping, and exploring the nearby Virgin Island and Balidbid Lagoon.The island offers a range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly resorts to more upscale properties.

Anika Island Resort

Anika Island Resort is a captivating eco-friendly resort located in Santa Fe, Bantayan Island, Cebu, Philippines. The resort, owned by a family who initially used the property for private gatherings, has evolved into a commercial venture that harmoniously blends modern amenities with environmental conservation efforts. Anika Island Resort stands out for its innovative use of up-cycled cargo containers to create air-conditioned rooms that are designed to withstand natural calamities like typhoons and earthquakes.

This eco-conscious approach reflects the owner's commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. The resort's lush tropical surroundings, powdery white sandy beaches, and azure waters provide a serene backdrop for guests seeking a tranquil escape from urban life. Anika Island Resort offers a range of experiences for guests to unwind and immerse themselves in the magic of a tropical paradise, making it a premier hideaway for individuals, couples, and groups of family and friends looking for a rejuvenating retreat.
