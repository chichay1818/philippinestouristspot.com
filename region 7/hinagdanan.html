Tourist Spot in Region 7

Hinagdanan Cave in Bohol

Hinagdanan Cave is a fascinating natural wonder located in Bohol, Philippines, known for its stunning limestone formations and crystal-clear underground pool. The cave gets its name from the Visayan word "hinagdanan," which means laddered, as it was originally accessed by a ladder. As you descend into the cave, you will be greeted by a surreal world of intricate stalactites and stalagmites hanging from the ceiling and rising from the floor, creating an otherworldly atmosphere that mesmerizes visitors.

The highlight of Hinagdanan Cave is its enchanting underground pool, illuminated by natural skylights that filter sunlight through openings in the cave's ceiling. The emerald-green waters of the pool invite visitors to take a refreshing dip and swim in this unique and picturesque setting. The cool and refreshing ambiance of the cave, combined with the tranquil beauty of the pool, creates a serene and peaceful environment that allows visitors to escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and connect with nature in a profound way.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Mithi Resort and Spa

Mithi Resort and Spa, formerly known as Panglao Island and Nature Resort, is a luxurious 4-star resort located in Panglao, Bohol, Philippines. Established in 1998, the resort spans 14 hectares and is renowned for its pristine white sand beach that stretches half a kilometer, offering guests a high level of privacy and seclusion. In 2016, the resort rebranded to Mithi Resort and Spa, maintaining its commitment to providing guests with a blissful vacation experience in Bohol's premier spa resort.

The resort offers various room types, including apartments, triples, and doubles, all equipped with modern amenities like minibars, safety deposit boxes, air conditioning, and hot tubs.Guests can enjoy a range of activities such as sun lounging, swimming, kayaking, paddleboarding, tennis, and fitness gym sessions.The iconic Dream Islet within the resort's landscape serves as a picturesque venue for private events, romantic dinners, and celebrations.

BE Grand Resort Bohol

BE Grand Resort Bohol is a luxurious 4-star resort located on the exclusive Panglao Island in Bohol, Philippines. The resort offers a blissful and tranquil retreat for guests, combining contemporary design with a laid-back waterfront ambiance.The property is located around 1 km from various attractions and amenities.

The resort has received positive reviews from guests, who have praised its serene surroundings, private beach, top-notch facilities, and luxurious accommodations.BE Grand Resort Bohol is known for its engaging service, modern design, and infusion of local Bohol flair, providing a memorable and relaxing vacation experience.
