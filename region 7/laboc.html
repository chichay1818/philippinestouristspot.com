Tourist Spot in Region 7

The Loboc River Cruise

The Loboc River Cruise in Bohol, Philippinesis a popular tourist attraction known for its scenic beauty, cultural experience, and unique dining offerings. The Loboc River Cruise offers visitors a leisurely boat ride along the tranquil Loboc River, which flows through lush green landscapes, coconut groves, and local villages. The cruise provides a peaceful and immersive experience in nature, allowing guests to relax and enjoy the picturesque scenery while sailing down the river.The Loboc River Cruise has become a popular tourist activity in Bohol over the years, attracting visitors from around the world to experience its unique blend of nature, culture, and cuisine. The cruise originated as a way for tourists to explore the scenic beauty of the Loboc River and immerse themselves in the local culture of Bohol.

The cruise operators have worked to enhance the experience by introducing cultural performances, traditional music, and dance shows, as well as serving delicious local food on board, making it a comprehensive and enjoyable experience for visitors.The Loboc River Cruise typically lasts around an hour or two, depending on the selected tour package, and is guided by experienced boatmen who share insightful commentary about the history and significance of the river and its surrounding areas. As the boat glides along the gentle currents, passengers can marvel at the stunning natural scenery, including cascading waterfalls, verdant forests, and an abundance of wildlife.

The cottages feature Filipino traditional architecture with modern amenities like fan-cooled rooms, mosquito nets, and hot/cold showers.
