Tourist Spot in Region 7

Oslob Whale Sharks

Oslob, a coastal city in the Philippines,has gained international attention for its whale shark tourism. The practice of provisioning or feeding whale sharks to attract them to the waters has been a topic of debate, with both positive and negative impacts on the local economy and the well-being of the whale sharks.Oslob's whale shark tourism has been a significant contributor to the local economy, generating income and job opportunities for the residents. The influx of tourists has also led to the development of infrastructure, such as hotels, restaurants, which has boosted economic growth and improved living standards for the local population.

The experience typically begins early in the morning, where visitors receive a briefing before boarding outrigger boats that take them to the interaction area. Once there, visitors are allowed to swim with the whale sharks for a limited time of 30 minutes, adhering to strict rules that include no touching, no riding, no flash photography, and keeping a safe distance of at least 2 meters away from the sharks. Diving with whale sharks is also possible at an additional surcharge.

Eden Resort

Eden Resort, located in Santander, Cebu, Philippines, is a luxurious beachfront retreat offering a tranquil and picturesque setting for travelers. The resort boasts an array of facilities and amenities, including an outdoor swimming pool, spa, restaurant, private beach area, and water sports activities like diving and snorkeling. Guests can enjoy comfortable accommodations with modern amenities such as air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and private balconies with ocean views.

The resort's restaurant serves a delectable spread of local and Western dishes, with options for special diet meals and room service. Additionally, services like motorbike/car rentals, spa treatments, tour arrangements, and airport transfers are available for guests' convenience. Eden Resort's serene location, excellent facilities, and attentive staff make it a highly recommended destination for a relaxing and enjoyable stay in Santander, Cebu.
