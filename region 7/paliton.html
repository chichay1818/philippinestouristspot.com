Tourist Spot in Region 7

Paliton Beach

Paliton Beach is a stunning beach located in San Juan, Siquijor Island, Philippines. It is known for its glistening white sands and palm tree-lined shoreline, making it a perfect spot to relax and unwind. The beach is situated on the western side of the island, which allows visitors to enjoy a breathtaking sunset view. Here's a detailed essay about Paliton Beach.

Paliton Beach is situated in the main tourist area of San Juan, which is easily accessible by scooter, habal-habal, tricycle, or on foot. Visitors can rent scooters directly from their accommodations for around 500 PHP per day, making it an affordable and convenient way to explore the island. The beach is also within walking distance from most accommodations in San Juan.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

White Villas Resort

White Villas Resort, located in the picturesque San Juan area of Siquijor Island, is a tropical paradise offering a serene and luxurious getaway for travelers. This small, cozy, and quiet beach resort is known for its commitment to ethical tourism, investing in local livelihoods, farming with nature, and promoting biodiversity. The resort provides a range of amenities and activities for guests, including a restaurant by the sea open from 7 am to 10 pm, free Wi-Fi, a small swimming pool, a sunset beach bar, free kayaks, and arrangements for motorbike rentals and island tours. While the beach at the resort may not be ideal for swimming, popular swimming beaches are nearby.

The resort's sunset views are often spectacular, providing a perfect backdrop for relaxation. White Villas Resort is a non-smoking property with a focus on sustainability, minimizing single-use plastics, using low-energy solutions, and growing organic products. Additionally, the resort supports local staff and their families, contributing to the community's well-being. With its proximity to cafes, restaurants, ATMs, and dive shops, White Villas Resort offers a peaceful and hospitable environment for guests to enjoy the beauty of Siquijor Island.

Infinity Heights Resort

Infinity Heights Resort is a resort located on the beautiful and mystical island of Siquijor in the Philippines. The resort is known for its stunning views, comfortable accommodations, and range of amenities and activities.

The resort offers a variety of room types, including standard rooms, deluxe rooms, and suites, all equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, private bathrooms, and balconies or terraces. The rooms are designed to provide a comfortable and relaxing stay for guests.
