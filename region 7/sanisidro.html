Tourist Spot in Region 7

San Isidro Labrador Church

The church's architecture is a testament to the skilled craftsmanship of its builders. The exterior features a pink-coral-stone facade, while the interior boasts impressive wooden elements, including the original wooden flooring, pulpits, and ceiling. These well-preserved features contribute to the church's timeless and nostalgic ambiance, transporting visitors to a bygone era.The San Isidro Labrador Church is a popular attraction for both local and international visitors to Siquijor. The church's serene and well-preserved atmosphere invites visitors to step back in time and appreciate the island's rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the church's interior, admire the architectural details, and engage with the caretakers or local residents to learn more about the church's history and significance.

The San Isidro Labrador Church has been recognized for its historical and cultural importance. In 1984, it was declared a National Historical Landmark by the National Historical Commission of the Philippines. Additionally, in 2012, the church was declared a National Cultural Treasure by the National Museum of the Philippines, highlighting its exceptional architectural and historical value.Despite the passage of time, the San Isidro Labrador Church has been well-maintained, with efforts to preserve its original features and character. The wooden flooring, for instance, has been meticulously cared for, making it a rare example of a church in the Philippines that has retained its original wooden flooring.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Casa De La Playa Beach Resort

Casa de la Playa Beach Resort, located in Sandugan, Larena, Siquijor, Philippines, is a beachfront resort offering a serene and picturesque retreat for travelers. The resort features a spa, restaurant, and accommodations overlooking the sea, providing guests with a tranquil setting to unwind and enjoy the beauty of Siquijor Island. With vibrant and cozy cottages and villas, guests can relax in comfort and style, with amenities like private balconies, air conditioning, and sofa beds.

The Pagoda Verde Restaurant at the resort serves a variety of local, international, and vegetarian dishes, ensuring a delightful dining experience. Guests can also enjoy activities like snorkeling, boating, and hiking, arranged by the tour desk staff. The resort's beachfront location, free Wi-Fi, and serene ambiance make it a popular choice for those seeking a peaceful and rejuvenating escape in Siquijor.

Lazi Beach Club

Lazi Beach Club Resort, located in Lazi, Siquijor, Philippines, is a popular beachfront resort offering a range of accommodations and amenities for a memorable vacation experience. The resort boasts a swimming pool, private beach area, and beautifully landscaped gardens, providing guests with a tranquil and relaxing environment to unwind. With various room options like beachfront rooms and cottages, the resort caters to different group sizes and preferences, ensuring a comfortable stay for all guests.

Additionally, Lazi Beach Club Resort offers package tours and adventures, including Siquijor Coastal Tours, Mountainous Tours, and kayaking experiences, allowing guests to explore the natural wonders of Siquijor Island. The resort's full-service bar, lounge, and bistro provide a delightful dining experience with daily lunch and dinner options, happy hour specials, and weekend entertainment. For those interested in marine exploration, the resort offers diving and snorkeling experiences with an in-house Marine Biologist to discover the fascinating marine life in the area.
