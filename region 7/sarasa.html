Tourist Spot in Region 7

Sarrasa International Hotel in Bohol

Sarrosa International Hotel offers modern accommodation a 5-minute drive from Cebu's commercial district. It features an indoor pool. Free parking at the hotel's basement carpark is available.Spacious rooms are fitted with wooden furnishings, Wi-Fi access and carpeted floors. They are equipped with cable TV and comfortable armchair with floor lamp. Large bathrooms have a bathtub and separate shower area.Sarrosa Hotel boasts a range of facilities and services to cater to the needs of every guest. From the inviting swimming pool and well-equipped fitness center to the indulgent spa and business center, the hotel offers a comprehensive experience that combines relaxation and productivity. The friendly and attentive staff are committed to delivering exceptional service to ensure that guests have a memorable and enjoyable stay at Sarrosa International Hotel and Residential Suites in Cebu.

Guests can choose from a selection of well-appointed rooms and suites that are designed to provide a peaceful retreat amidst the bustling city. Each accommodation option at Sarrosa Hotel features contemporary furnishings, plush bedding, modern amenities, and stunning views of the city or the hotel's serene surroundings. Whether relaxing in the spacious rooms or unwinding in the lavish suites, guests are guaranteed a restful and rejuvenating stay.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Alpa City Suites

Alpa City Suites is a hotel located in Cebu, Philippines, offering comfortable accommodations and modern amenities for guests. The hotel provides a range of services and facilities to ensure a pleasant stay, including well-appointed rooms, a restaurant, and various amenities to cater to the needs of travelers. Guests can enjoy a convenient and relaxing experience at Alpa City Suites in Cebu.

Guests mentioned that the rooms at Alpa City Suites were quiet, comfortable, and the staff was eager to please.The breakfast buffet was highlighted as excellent by guests.Some guests noted good service from the friendly staff, especially in the restaurant during breakfast. However, there were suggestions that the breakfast choices could have been better.

Citi Park Hotel

Citi Park Hotel is a well-regarded mid-range hotel located in Cebu City, Philippines, offering guests a comfortable and convenient stay. Situated between Mandaue and Cebu, the hotel is just a 10-minute drive from popular shopping destinations like SM Malls and Ayala Malls. The hotel features a restaurant serving buffet breakfast and Asian dishes, as well as a bar with live band performances. Guests can enjoy air-conditioned rooms equipped with modern amenities like flat-screen satellite TVs, safes, tea/coffee makers, and work desks.

The hotel provides services such as laundry, room service, and a 24-hour front desk. Additionally, Citi Park Hotel offers free WiFi, family rooms, free parking, and non-smoking rooms for the comfort of its guests. Overall, the hotel has received positive reviews for its friendly staff, clean rooms, good food, and convenient location, making it a popular choice for travelers visiting Cebu City.
