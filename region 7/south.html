Tourist Spot in Region 7

South Palms Resort

South Palms Resort in Bohol is a luxurious beachfront resort located on Panglao Island. The resort offers stunning views of the pristine white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters of the Bohol Sea. Guests can relax and unwind in one of the resort's spacious and beautifully designed rooms, each offering modern amenities and elegant furnishings.

With its breathtaking natural beauty, luxurious accommodations, and top-notch amenities, South Palms Resort in Bohol is the perfect destination for a relaxing getaway or a romantic escape. Whether you're looking to soak up the sun on the beach, explore the vibrant marine life of the nearby reefs, or simply unwind in a tranquil setting, this resort offers everything you need for a memorable vacation in paradise.
