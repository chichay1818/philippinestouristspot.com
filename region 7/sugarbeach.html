Tourist Spot in Region 7

Sugar Beach

Sugar Beach: A Tropical Oasis in the Heart of the Caribbean.Nestled between the iconic Piton mountains on the island of St. Lucia, Sugar Beach is a true tropical paradise. This luxury resort, set on 100 acres of lush rainforest, offers guests an unparalleled experience of Caribbean bliss.Beyond the luxurious villas, Sugar Beach offers a wealth of amenities and activities to delight its guests. The main resort area boasts multiple restaurants, each with its own unique culinary personality. From the fresh seafood and wood-fired pizzas at Bayside to the Asian-inspired delicacies at The Cane Bar, the dining options are nothing short of exceptional. The resort's breakfast buffet is a true highlight, featuring a diverse array of freshly baked breads, tropical juices, and made-to-order smoothies.

The resort's accommodations are nothing short of breathtaking. The villas, designed with a colonial-inspired aesthetic, feature expansive patios that provide front-row seats to the most stunning sunsets imaginable. Gauzy mosquito netting adorns the four-poster beds, creating a dreamy, serene ambiance. Every window in the villas faces the sparkling azure waters of the Caribbean Sea, where guests can watch chartered catamarans and private yachts drift in and out of the harbor.
