REGION 7 Tourist Spot

Tourist Spot in Region 7

Willy's Rock'

Willy’s Rock is arguably Boracay’s most photographed landmark. It’s a rocky outcrop that forms a tidal island with a statue of the Virgin Mary on top of steps that are carved into the volcanic rock. Willy’s Rock gets its name from the nearby Willy’s Beach Club Hotel and is situated near Station 1 on Boracay’s 4 km stretch of white sandy beach.

Embark on an unforgettable journey as you set out to visit the captivating Manjuyod Sandbar, a hidden gem nestled near Dumaguete City. As the sun rises over the tranquil North Bais Bay, the sandbar emerges like a pristine oasis, earning its reputation as the "Maldives of the Philippines." This enchanting paradise, known for its vast stretches of powdery white sand, beckons you to a world of wonder and relaxation. As low tide unveils the sandbar's splendor, embark on an idyllic dolphin-watching experience, witnessing these playful creatures in their natural habitat. With the gentle waves lapping against the shore, you can indulge in thrilling banana boat rides or simply bask in the sun's warmth on the sandbar's serene expanse.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Willy’s Beach Club

Willy’s Beach Club Hotel is a beachfront resort in Station 1, right across the iconic ‘Willy’s Rock”. It is a wholesome family destination and great wedding venue because of its proximity to Willy’s Rock and the church plaza, as well as the most economical full-fledged hotel among the resorts in the area. Apart from the picturesque location, they also take pride in their 38 well maintained rooms, Becca’s Cafe & Restaurant and the Danzka Bar where guests can enjoy the beach view and the famous Boracay sunset on their beach lounge chairs. Room amenities include air conditioning, hot and cold shower, cable TV, telephone, electric kettle, minibar, and WiFi Internet access in public areas.

Henann Prime Beach Resort

Henann Prime Beach Resort is a premier beachfront resort located in the prime Station 1 area of Boracay Island, Philippines. This resort, part of the renowned Henann Group of Resorts, offers guests a luxurious and relaxing experience in one of the world's most beautiful island destinations.Rooms are equipped with essential amenities such as air conditioning, television, and private bathrooms. Some rooms even offer direct pool access or balconies with stunning sea views, allowing guests to fully immerse themselves in the resort's picturesque setting.

Beyond the well-appointed rooms, Henann Prime Beach Resort offers a range of facilities and services to cater to the diverse needs of its guests. The resort's outdoor swimming pool runs along the length of the property, providing a refreshing oasis for guests to cool off and relax. The on-site Sea Salt Café serves a delectable selection of local and international cuisine, catering to the culinary preferences of both local and international travelers.
