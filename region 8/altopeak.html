Tourist Spots in Region 8




Alto Peak

Alto Peak, located in the province of Leyte, stands at an elevation of approximately 1,332 meters (4,370 feet) above sea level. The peak is part of the scenic Alto Peak National Park, which features lush forests, diverse flora and fauna, and stunning panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.


Alto Peak is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, offering opportunities for hiking, trekking, and nature exploration.


The climb to Alto Peak is moderately challenging, with trails winding through dense forests and steep slopes. However, the breathtaking views from the summit make the effort worthwhile.

Nearby Hotels

Asia Stars Hotel - Location, Baybay City, Leyte. Another mid-range option in Baybay City, offering clean and spacious rooms. It provides a convenient base for travelers exploring Alto Peak and its surroundings.


These accommodations offer various options to suit different budgets and preferences, providing a comfortable place to stay while exploring Alto Peak and the surrounding areas.
