Tourist Spots in Region 8


The Biri Rock Formations

The Biri Rock Formations are a geological wonder located in Biri Island, part of the province of Northern Samar in the Philippines. These formations are characterized by massive rock pillars, natural arches, and unique shapes sculpted by the elements over thousands of years. They are a popular destination for tourists and photographers, offering breathtaking views of the rugged coastline and the Pacific Ocean.


The rock formations include several notable landmarks such as the Magasang, Magsapad, and Macadlaw rock formations. Visitors can explore these formations by walking along designated trails and viewpoints.
