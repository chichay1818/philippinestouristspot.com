Tourist Spots in Region 8

Calicoan Island, located in Eastern Samar, Region 8 of the Philippines, is a breathtaking destination renowned for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and world-class surfing waves. This picturesque island boasts stunning landscapes, with powdery white sands stretching along its shores and lush greenery adorning its interior. Calicoan Island is a haven for water sports enthusiasts, offering excellent opportunities for surfing, snorkeling, and diving amidst its vibrant marine life.
In addition to its natural beauty, Calicoan Island also features luxurious resorts and accommodations that cater to the needs of travelers seeking relaxation and adventure. Visitors can indulge in beachfront villas, cozy cottages, and modern amenities while immersing themselves in the tranquil ambiance of the island. Whether lounging by the pool, exploring the underwater wonders of its marine sanctuaries, or embarking on island-hopping adventures, Calicoan Island offers a paradise retreat for vacationers seeking an unforgettable tropical getaway.
