Tourist Spots in Region 8


Canhugas Nature Park

Canhugas Nature Park is a serene natural attraction located in the municipality of Babatngon, Leyte, Philippines. It is known for its lush greenery, refreshing springs, and picturesque landscape, offering visitors a tranquil escape into nature.


Visitors can explore the park's trails, relax in shaded areas, take a dip in the springs, and enjoy a picnic amidst the natural beauty. The park offers a peaceful retreat for families, nature enthusiasts, and outdoor adventurers alike.


Nearby Hotels and Accomodation

Ormoc City: Ormoc City is the nearest major city to Babatngon and offers a range of hotel and accommodation options to suit various budgets and preferences. It's located about an hour's drive from Babatngon, making it a convenient base for exploring Canhugas Nature Park and other attractions in the region.


Tacloban City: Tacloban City, the capital of Leyte province, is another option for accommodation. While it's a bit farther from Babatngon compared to Ormoc City, it offers a wider range of hotels, inns, and guesthouses catering to different tastes and budgets.


It's always a good idea to book your accommodation in advance, especially in remote locations like this, to ensure availability. Additionally, make sure to check for updated information and reviews online before making your reservations.
