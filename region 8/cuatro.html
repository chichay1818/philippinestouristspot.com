The Cuatro Islas (Four Islands)

The Cuatro Islas (Four Islands) are a group of stunning islands located in the Leyte Gulf, off the coast of Leyte province in the Philippines. These islands are known for their pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, making them a popular destination for beach lovers, snorkelers, and adventurers.


The Cuatro Islas consist of four main islands: Digyo, Mahaba, Apid, and Himokilan. Each island offers its own unique charms, from secluded coves to vibrant coral reefs.


Visitors to the Cuatro Islas can enjoy a variety of activities, including swimming, snorkeling, island hopping, beachcombing, and picnicking. The islands are also great for camping, with some areas designated for overnight stays.


Nearby Hotels and Accomodation

As for accommodations on or near The Cuatro Islas, options might be limited due to its relatively remote location. However, there are a few places you might consider:


Leyte Park Resort Hotel: Located in Tacloban City, Leyte Park Resort Hotel is a popular accommodation choice for visitors to Leyte province. While it's not right next to the Cuatro Islas, it offers comfortable rooms, amenities such as swimming pools and restaurants, and easy access to transportation.


It's always a good idea to book your accommodation in advance, especially in remote locations like this, to ensure availability. Additionally, make sure to check for updated information and reviews online before making your reservations.
