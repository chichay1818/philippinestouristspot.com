Mac Arthur Landing Memorial National Park

Mac Arthur Landing Memorial National Park located in Brgy. Candahug, Palo, Leyte, Philippines. -The park features a large bronze statue of General Douglas MacArthur wading ashore, along with other monuments and markers commemorating the events of the Leyte Landing. Visitors can explore the park, learn about the history of the landing through informational displays, and pay homage to the bravery and sacrifices of those who fought in the war.


Visitors can take guided tours of the park, visit the various monuments and exhibits, and enjoy the scenic views of Leyte Gulf. The park also hosts ceremonies and events to commemorate significant anniversaries and occasions related to the Leyte Landing.
