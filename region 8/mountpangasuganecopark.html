Tourist Spots in Region 8




Mount Pangasugan Ecopark

Mount Pangasugan Ecopark, located in Garcia Hernandez, Bohol, Philippines. The ecopark offers visitors a chance to explore nature through activities such as hiking, birdwatching, and picnicking. The park boasts beautiful landscapes, including lush forests, rolling hills, and panoramic views.


Hiking trails lead visitors through the park's diverse ecosystems, providing opportunities for nature observation and photography. There are also designated picnic areas where visitors can relax and enjoy the scenery. Birdwatching is another popular activity, as the park is home to a variety of bird species.


Mount Pangasugan Ecopark provides basic amenities such as restrooms and picnic shelters. However, visitors are encouraged to bring their own food and drinks.

Nearby Hotels

While there may not be hotels directly at Mount Pangasugan Ecopark, there are accommodations available in nearby towns where travelers can stay. Here are some options


Alona Kew White Beach Resort - is a beachfront property offering comfortable accommodations and modern amenities. Guests can enjoy access to a private beach, swimming pool, restaurant, and spa services.


Bohol South Beach Hotel is a boutique hotel situated along the shores of Bolod Beach. The hotel features stylish rooms with ocean views, a swimming pool, restaurant, and bar. Guests can also partake in various water sports activities arranged by the hotel.


These accommodations offer various options to suit different budgets and preferences, providing a comfortable place to stay while exploring Mount Pangasugan Ecopark and the surrounding areas. It's advisable to book accommodations in advance, especially during peak travel seasons. Additionally, travelers should inquire about transportation options to reach Mount Pangasugan Ecopark from their chosen accommodation.
