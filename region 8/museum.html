Tourist Spots in Region 8


The Santo Niño Shrine and Heritage Museum

The shrine and museum complex consists of the grand mansion itself, adorned with opulent furnishings, intricate woodwork, and valuable artworks. Visitors can explore various rooms showcasing the Marcos family's possessions, as well as exhibits highlighting Philippine history, art, and culture.


Notable attractions within the museum include the Imelda Romualdez Marcos Hall, the Ferdinand E. Marcos Presidential Hall, and the Marcos Memorabilia Room, which display personal effects, gifts, and souvenirs collected by the Marcoses during their time in power.
