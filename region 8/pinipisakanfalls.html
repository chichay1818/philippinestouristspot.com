Tourist Spots in Region 8




Pinipisakan Falls

Pinipisakan Falls is nestled within the lush forests of Calbiga, Samar, in the Eastern Visayas region of the Philippines. The falls are renowned for their picturesque beauty, with crystal-clear waters cascading down rugged cliffs into a tranquil pool below. Surrounded by verdant vegetation, Pinipisakan Falls offers visitors a serene and refreshing escape into nature.


Visitors can enjoy swimming in the cool waters of the falls' pool, picnicking along the banks, and immersing themselves in the natural beauty of the surroundings. The area also provides opportunities for trekking and exploring the nearby forests.


Pinipisakan Falls is accessible by land transportation, typically via private vehicles or local modes such as tricycles or motorcycles for hire. The journey involves traveling along scenic roads before embarking on a short hike to reach the falls.
