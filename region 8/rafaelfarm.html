Rafael Farm

Rafael Farm offers visitors a unique blend of agricultural experiences, natural beauty, and recreational activities in a serene rural setting. The farm boasts lush greenery, rolling hills, and scenic landscapes, providing a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.


Visitors to Rafael Farm can enjoy a variety of activities, including farm tours, organic farming workshops, fruit picking, and leisurely walks through the sprawling grounds.The farm also features charming cottages, picnic areas, and open spaces where visitors can relax, unwind, and immerse themselves in nature.


Rafael Farm is known for its warm hospitality, delicious farm-to-table meals, and opportunities for guests to connect with local culture and traditions.
