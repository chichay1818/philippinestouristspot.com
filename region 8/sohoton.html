Sohoton Natural Bridge National Park

Sohoton Natural Bridge National Park is situated in Basey, Samar, in the Eastern Visayas region of the Philippines. The park is renowned for its magnificent natural limestone formations, particularly the Sohoton Natural Bridge, which spans across the Sohoton River. Visitors to the park can marvel at the breathtaking scenery, explore the surrounding caves and rock formations, and enjoy activities such as river cruising, kayaking, and spelunking.


Popular activities in Sohoton Natural Bridge National Park include boat tours along the Sohoton River, where visitors can pass through the natural bridge and witness the beauty of the limestone cliffs and emerald-green waters. The park also offers opportunities for swimming, cliff jumping, and exploring the enchanting Sohoton Cave Complex.


Sohoton Natural Bridge National Park is accessible by land transportation from Tacloban City or other nearby towns in Samar. From the town of Basey, visitors can arrange boat tours to explore park's attractions, including the natural bridge, caves, and river.
