Tourist Spots in Region 8




Tinago Falls

Tinago Falls is nestled amidst lush greenery, surrounded by towering cliffs and dense foliage, creating a picturesque and serene natural setting. The waterfall cascades from a height of around 73 meters (240 feet) into a deep, emerald-colored pool, creating a stunning sight and providing a refreshing oasis for visitors.


To reach Tinago Falls, visitors typically embark on a short trek through a forested trail, followed by descending a steep staircase comprising around 500 steps, adding to the adventure and allure of the destination.


Once at the base of Tinago Falls, visitors can swim in the cool, crystalline waters, marvel at the natural beauty of the surroundings, and even explore behind the cascading veil of the waterfall.
