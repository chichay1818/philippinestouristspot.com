Tourist Spot in Region 9

Dipolog Boulevard

The Dipolog Boulevard, also known as the Dipolog Sunset Boulevard or Foreshore Development and Wellness Center, is an esplanade in Dipolog, Zamboanga del Norte, Philippines. It spans a 7.6-kilometer stretch of foreshore along the city, offering a scenic and recreational area for locals and tourists. The boulevard features amenities like basketball courts, playgrounds, commercial buildings, restaurants, bars, and food and beverage peddlers. It serves as a venue for various events such as the "P'gsalabuk Festival," motor company trade shows, marathons, and triathlons.

The city government has plans to extend the boulevard further, creating a river park esplanade and developing areas beyond the Dipolog Airport. The project aims to enhance the city's infrastructure, housing, social services, and health services as part of the SWIGAPORE 2030 program. The Dipolog Boulevard has become a popular leisure destination, attracting visitors for walks, family outings, festivals, and other recreational activities.
