Tourist Spot in Region 9

Buluan Island

Buluan Island is a famous beach destination among Sibugaynons and is now slowly gaining popularity in nearby towns and provinces.Buluan Island features white sand beach and clear blue waters. The uncombed sand may not be super powdery fine but Buluan Island can offer seclusion and tranquility. From the cottages, you can enjoy the fresh sea breeze while admiring the view of the highlan.

The best months to visit is between March-May and September-November when the waves are much calmer. Avoid the months of June-August as this is when heavy rainfalls are expected.The Buluan Island is beautiful. There are many activities you can do: Swimming, Snorkeling, Enjoying the views, Cruising the island. If you plan to stay during the night, make sure to set up your tents inside the military area for your safety and protection. You can actually enjoy more by setting up a bonfire. That would be very ideal for bonding with friends.

In Zamboanga Sibugay is a cream colored island that will soothe your beach lover dreams. With the cool and turquoise blue waters, your stress will go away when you go swimming on the beach! Buluan Island, located in Zamboanga Sibugay, is a gift of nature that keeps on giving. Spend a day in paradise in this hidden piece of nature. If you want to enjoy a quiet day at the beach with little to no people around you, then this one is worth a shot.
