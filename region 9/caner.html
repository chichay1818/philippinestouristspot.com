Canelar Barter Trade Center

Are you looking for a place to buy souvenirs? Then the Canelar Barter Trade Center is the perfect place for you. This place will test your ability to haggle and pick which items to buy with its assortment of stores and products. Nearby Hotel Accommodation

LM Metro Hotel

Welcome to LM Metro Hotel, a 3-star hotel located in the vibrant city of Zamboanga in the Philippines. With its prime location and excellent amenities, this hotel is the perfect choice for both business and leisure travelers. Upon arrival, guests are greeted by a warm and friendly staff, ready to assist with any needs or inquiries. Check-in begins at 2:00 PM, allowing you to settle into your room and start your stay right away.

The hotel's sleek and modern design creates a welcoming atmosphere, while the well-appointed rooms provide a comfortable retreat after a long day of exploring the city. With a total of 33 rooms, LM Metro Hotel offers a cozy and intimate setting for guests. Each room is thoughtfully designed with modern furnishings and amenities to ensure a relaxing stay. Whether you choose a Standard Room or a Suite, you can expect a comfortable bed, a private bathroom, and all the necessary amenities to make you feel at home.

Lantaka Hotel

Lantaka Hotel by the Sea offers a wide range of entertainment facilities to ensure a memorable and enjoyable stay for its guests. Whether you are looking to relax, socialize, or indulge in some self-care, this hotel has something for everyone.

Step into the hotel's shops and explore a variety of local products and souvenirs, allowing you to bring a piece of Zamboanga City back home with you. The bar provides a vibrant and lively atmosphere, perfect for enjoying a refreshing drink or two while mingling with fellow travelers. For those in need of pampering, the salon offers a range of beauty treatments and services to help you feel rejuvenated. Indulge in ultimate relaxation at the hotel's massage center, where skilled therapists will work their magic to ease any tension or stress.
