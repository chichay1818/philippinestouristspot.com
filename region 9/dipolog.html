Dipolog Cathedral, canonically recognized as the Our Lady of the Most Holy Rosary Cathedral Parish, is a Roman Catholic church located in Estaka, Dipolog, Zamboanga del Norte in the Philippines. The cathedral is the seat of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Dipolog. It is situated in the city of Dipolog, the capital city of the Philippine province of Zamboanga del Norte, some 715 kilometres south of Manila. The church building is a fitting symbol for the church in Dipolog. Started to be built in 1893, it has undergone various renovations. Lately, the altar has been reconstructed. In 2021, the cathedral was designated as one of the 500 Jubilee Churches as part of the activities for the quincentennial of Philippine Christianity.
