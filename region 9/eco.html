Tourist Spot in Region 9

Cogon Eco-Tourism Park Campsite

The Cogon Eco-Tourism Park in Dipolog, Zamboanga Del Norte, is a popular destination known for its vast forest, diverse tree species like Acacia and Narra, and outdoor activities such as camping and trekking.Visitors can enjoy a mini-zoo with monkeys, birds, and other wildlife, as well as experience the thrill of climbing the tallest treehouse in Mindanao.

The park also features a replica of the Subanen Village, where tourists can interact with tribesmen, witness traditional dances, and learn about their culture and crafts, like basket weaving. To maintain the park's cleanliness and protect the environment, smoking is prohibited and littering is strictly prohibited, with staff ensuring compliance and safety.The local government collaborates with ecological groups to enhance the park, adding amenities like picnic cottages and an information centre to attract more visitors.
