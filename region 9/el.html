Tourist Spot in Region 9

El Museo De Zamboanga

Museo de Zamboanga should be the first stop of any city tour. It offers a quick overview of the history and heritage of Zamboanga City, which explains much of what you’ll see as you visit more places through the day.

It has two galleries — the main gallery, housing the permanent exhibition, and another one for the temporary or seasonal exhibits. It showcases historical items and artifacts that narrate the colorful story of T yhe region, its people, and its rich culture from the pre-colonial period to the more recent decades. The museum is nestled in the extensive Pasonanca Park.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Garden Orchid Hotel

The Garden Orchid Hotel is a hotel located in Zamboanga City, Philippines. It is situated near the airport and within a 5-minute drive of KCC Mall De Zamboanga and Metropolitan Cathedral of Immaculate Conception

The hotel offers a range of amenities, including an outdoor swimming pool, spa, massage, fitness room, parking, free airport pick-up service, and free restaurant4. Guests can choose from various room types, including Deluxe Double Room, Deluxe Room with Sofa bed, and Superior Room.

The hotel has received mixed reviews from travelers. Some guests have praised its cleanliness and location, while others have noted that the hotel's fixtures and facilities need updating.

The Ever O Business Hotel

The Ever O Business Hotel in Zamboanga City, Philippines is a 3-star hotel offering a range of facilities to ensure a comfortable stay. It is located in a good neighborhood, near the Zamboanga International Airport (ZAM), and within walking distance to popular landmarks like Mount Pulong Bato and Fort Pilar Shrine.The hotel provides 24-hour front desk service, room service, and free WiFi. It also offers a restaurant, airport shuttle service (for a fee), and parking. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TVs with cable channels, and private bathrooms with free toiletries

The hotel's prices vary depending on the room type and availability, but on average, a room costs around $62 USD5. The hotel does not accept reservations through its website at the moment but offers other nearby accommodations
