Great Santa Crus Island

Famous for its pink sand beach, Great Santa Cruz Island in Zamboanga City is a popular destination for beach lovers. The unique pink hue of the sand comes from crushed red organ-pipe coral mixed with white sand.

Great Santa Cruz Island, located in Zamboanga City, Philippines, is renowned for its pink coralline sand, a unique feature resulting from pulverized red organ pipe coral mixed with white sand. This small island is part of the Great and Little Sta.

Cruz Islands Protected Landscape & Seascape, emphasizing conservation and ecotourism. Visitors can enjoy the pristine beach, lush scenery, and marine life while adhering to strict rules against damaging the environment. Accessible by boat from Paseo del Mar, the island offers day tours from 7 AM to 2 PM, showcasing its natural beauty and biodiversity.
