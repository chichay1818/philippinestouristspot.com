Tourist Spot in Region 9

Mampang Blue Lagoon

Hidden in Zamboanga del Sur is an affordable but beautiful paradise that is the Blue Lagoon. Located in Brgy. Baganian, Tabina – this beach destination is sought after by locals and is getting recognition from other Mindanao locals!The place is a perfect paradise for people who love the sea. Their water is crystal clear, they have intact coral trenches teeming with marine life, and the place is an untouched wonder of nature that is raw and bare. If you want to go to tourist destinations but want to avoid the crowd, then Blue Lagoon is perfect for you.

The place is two-piece ready for picture takings and instagram shots. You can slay with normal clothes but let’s be extra. Awesome and comfortable outfits can step up your game. Known for its mesmerizing blue-green waters, the lagoon is a great spot for swimming and picnics. Its natural beauty offers a perfect backdrop for nature photography.
