Tourist Spot in Region 9

Merloquet Falls

Located in Zamboanga City, Merloquet Falls is a picturesque waterfall nestled amidst lush greenery. It's a favorite spot for nature lovers and adventurers who enjoy trekking and swimming in natural pools

Merloquet Falls in Zamboanga is a picturesque destination with a 10-15 minute descent down 370 steps to reach the falls, offering pools and rock ledges for visitors to enjoy. The area provides parking for a fee, clean toilet facilities, and a scenic experience suitable for picnics and swimming. 

Visitors can hire private vans or take a bus to Vitali, then a habal-habal to the falls, with entrance fees of P5 per person and parking fees ranging from P10 to P20. It's recommended to bring packed lunch, proper footwear, and extra clothes for a day trip to Merloquet Falls.
