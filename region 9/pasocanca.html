Tourist Spot in Region 9

Pasocanca Park & ScoutLimbaga Campsite

Pasonanca Park & Scout Limbaga Campsite Opened to the public in the 1910s, the Pasonanca Park is another green space for nature lovers and those who want to have a quick escape from the much busier city center.

It houses the Camp Limbaga (Scout Limbaga Campsite) with a convention center, an amphitheater, an aviary, a floral garden and butterfly sanctuary, and public swimming pools — a kiddie pool, a natural pool, and an Olympic-size pool.

Nearby Hotel Accommodation

Garden Orchid Hotel

The Garden Orchid Hotel is a hotel located in Zamboanga City, Philippines. It is situated near the airport and within a 5-minute drive of KCC Mall De Zamboanga and Metropolitan Cathedral of Immaculate Conception

The hotel offers a range of amenities, including an outdoor swimming pool, spa, massage, fitness room, parking, free airport pick-up service, and free restaurant4. Guests can choose from various room types, including Deluxe Double Room, Deluxe Room with Sofa bed, and Superior Room.

The hotel has received mixed reviews from travelers. Some guests have praised its cleanliness and location, while others have noted that the hotel's fixtures and facilities need updating.

The Ever O Business Hotel

The Ever O Business Hotel in Zamboanga City, Philippines is a 3-star hotel offering a range of facilities to ensure a comfortable stay. It is located in a good neighborhood, near the Zamboanga International Airport (ZAM), and within walking distance to popular landmarks like Mount Pulong Bato and Fort Pilar Shrine

The hotel provides 24-hour front desk service, room service, and free WiFi. It also offers a restaurant, airport shuttle service (for a fee), and parking. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TVs with cable channels, and private bathrooms with free toiletries
