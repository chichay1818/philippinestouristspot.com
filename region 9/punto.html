Punto de Dissembarco

Punto is 10 feet tall and the monument valued 35 million marks the spot where he first set foot many years ago in Dapitan. It is an elevated semi-circular structure and has the area of 1,500 sq m. It has both aesthetic and functional purposes. At the raised center is the human life in size statues.The figures are: Dr. Jose P. Rizal, Capt. Delgras, the SS Cebu skipper, and the three artillery men waving from the sea going to Casa Real using Farol de Combateas their lights. Both sides are promenading areas with bromite flooring finished and enclosed with art design concrete balustrade with granite nails.

The whole project was secured with shorelines protection composed of steel file and stone masonry riprap. The historical landmark is funded by the Local Government Unit of Dapitan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte and by donations from prominent personalities who valued the ideals, teachings, courage, and sacrifices of our national hero. Ground breaking ceremony took place on December 29, 2009.
