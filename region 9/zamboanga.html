Tourist Spot in Region 9

Zamboanga City Hall

Known for its stunning architecture, the Zamboanga City Hall is a must-visit for history and culture enthusiasts. It features a blend of Spanish and modern Filipino architectural styles and is located in Plaza Rizal, which is also worth exploring.

Construction of the historic building was commenced in 1905 by the U.S. federal government and completed in 1907. It initially served as the official residence of the U.S. military governor of the Moro Province.The building later became the seat of government for the Department of Mindanao and Sulu, and the defunct Zamboanga province. Since 1937, it has functioned as the city hall of Zamboanga City.
